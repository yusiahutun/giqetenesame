    Steps to Use Essential Oils for Hair Growth

    Before you begin with the application, check the directions of usage mentioned on the packaging. Some essential oils for hair regrowth should be blended along with carrier oils whereas a few can be applied directly to the scalp only if the packaging explicitly says so. The most common practices of using an essential oil are as follows:

    1. For those who have dry hair, it is advisable to use carrier oils like argan, coconut, or avocado oil. These oils aid in moisturizing the scalp. 

    2. Mix 2- 3 drops of the essential oil into a tablespoon of carrier oil. 

    3. Blend the mixture well. 

    4. Put your fingertips into it and begin applying it.

    5. Apply the mixture to your scalp’s front, rubbing in circles.

    6. Massage gently for 2 to 3 minutes with your fingertips or a massaging comb. 

    7. Dip your fingers periodically and focus on the sides, the back, and the middle of the scalp and hair. 

    8. You may leave your hair oiled on overnight or rinse after an hour post-application. Please consider checking the directions for usage before applying the oil to your hair and scalp. 

    Tada, you are done!
