LeAnn Rimes Rocks One Super Sexy Angel Costume, Reveals Halloween Plans with Eddie &amp; Stepchildren

LeAnn Rimes just came straight outta heaven!

The "Can't Fight The Moonlight" singer looked like she walked off the Victoria's Secret runway when she hosted Life & Style's Eye Candy Halloween Bash in Los Angeles on Thursday, dressed as a super sexy angel.

The 33-year-old wowed in a white embellished bra and matching corset she paired with boy shorts and a tiny, sheer tutu. She kept her hair in glam waves and accessorized with bronze makeup, light pink pumps and of course a pair of gorgeous angel wings.

The country cutie chatted with toofab's Candice Brock at the event and gave us all the details about the inspiration behind her sexy look and her big Halloween plans with husband Eddie Cibrian!

"Well, the party's called 'Eye Candy,' so I had this genius idea to dress up as an angel," she laughed. "Not till I started trying on lingerie yesterday did I realize how hard these women must work their asses off. I work out, but I have a whole new respect!"

"I'm like, 'Oh my god, this is skimpy and I have to walk a red carpet in this,' but you know, what I'm rocking it and I'm having a good time, so it's very fun!"

LeAnn showed up to the event solo, but the star did open up about her weekend plans with her hunky hubby and two stepsons, Mason and Jake ... the two boys he shares with ex-wife Brandi Glanville.

"We have the boys, so my stepsons are going to be with us for Halloween, our neighborhood does like a huge Halloween party and the whole deal so we're going to be trick or treating with them," she explained.
