Sarah Mauro Bio, Age, Height, Husband, Net Worth

Sarah Mauro is the driving force behind the famous chef Jeff Mauro who is known extensively for his influential personality in the industry of food networks. He was the winner of Season 7 of Food Network Star and has become the presenter of the shows on Food Network. In July 2022, he became the face of another competition series called Kitchen Crash.

He is not only famous for his weight loss story (that he lost through Crossfit) but has also wondered people behind the cameras. He is one of the most famous personalities, no doubt. Only a few know about his wife, Sarah, and son, Lorenzo. Both often appeared on Jeff’s food shows or his competitions. Let’s learn about the personality traits of Jeff’s wife, Sarah Mauro.
