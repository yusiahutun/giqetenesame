After work, I love turning on Netflix and getting cozy with a soft blanket (or two). As an avid cuddler, I have many different blankets—some match my decor perfectly, while others I keep mainly because they’re super comfy. The only issue? Finding places to store all of them. You want blankets to be both easily accessible as well as easy to put away when you’re not using them. Throwing blankets into a linen closet isn’t always the best (or cutest) storage idea. Personally, I end up stuffing my blankets (and towels and sheets) onto the shelves, and then things inevitably fall out when I open the door. This is where blanket storage solutions come into play.

Whether you love to display your blankets around the house or keep them hidden, there are tons of blanket storage ideas to help you keep your home both cozy and tidy. From ladders to storage ottomans, the products below will keep your home clean and your favorite blanket within reach.
