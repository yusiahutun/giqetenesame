Happy 48th Birthday, Sarah Jessica Parker!

The actress is best known for playing fashionista Carrie Bradshaw on "Sex and the City." We're used to seeing SJP dressed up in the most extravagant designer clothes on screen, but off screen is a completely different story! One would expect the actress to take some fashion nods from her iconic character, but she apparently doesn't think she needs the fashion advice.

Remember the time she dressed paired her bra with a matador jacket? Or the time she couldn't move her arms? Or when she wore every '90s trend in one outfit: overalls, giant blazer, flower hat, beaded necklace AND peace pin?
