1. The knot braided hairstyles

This is arguably one of the fastest means you can get the curls in your knot braids. In case you are beginning with hair that has been freshly washed, apply the curl-enhancing wave spray. Massage the products thoroughly into the damp hair as you let it dry by itself. Start parting the hair in the natural way that you are used to. Take one of the sections of the hair from either side of the head towards the front of your eye level. Place the sections so that they all meet at the back of your head.

Tie the sections into regular knots with the hair at the back. Create another knot which is about half-inch below the first one. There should be a gap that develops between the two knots thus creating a shape of a circle. Ensure that you hold the hair in place with the use of the bobby pins.

Start working on the dripping wet hair immediately as you being with detangling it first. Detangling helps in elongating and stretching out the curls as far as possible. When the braids have dried up, you can maximize the length and also minimize the shrinkage. Here are the procedural steps afterwards:

Step 1: Section the curls

Pull back all the hair at the back, then with a comb or your hands divide them into sections. A comb is preferred since it really partitions the hair into the different sections. Pick up the section by the ears and divide them into two strand twists. On the other side, repeat the same process with more precision until all the hair has been sectioned. It is advised to twist the hair since it helps in maintaining the moisture as you progress to working on the other sections. This also prevents getting the hair dry and frizzing as you work on the other sections.

Step 2: Smoothing of the hair

With the use of your fingers in the middle section, the hair smooth while combing it at the back. Pick up a small section of the hair line and partition it into three parts and commence with the braiding. It is easy to go for a braid out as compared to going for a French braid. French braids are hard nut to crack since when they are released they give off more of a zigzag pattern when the results should be a curl.

Alternatively, you can utilize the Dutch braiding technique which is the same as used when making cornrows. When you finished off with the style it adds more lift to the scalp as opposed to the French braids which lay flat against the head.

Step 3: Braiding

Braiding is the tricky part when making the curls. You can simply overlap each of the three mini sections that you have created with one section of the hair at a time. If you are going for a Dutch braid or the corn row, ensure that the middle section goes over from the outside sections. Place each of the section of the hair to the middle position at least once; also go for the next cross over as you add more hair to the outside piece.

Also, overlap the middle piece over that section as you repeat with the outside piece on the remaining side. Proceed down with the scalp until the pieces run out so that you can add more. Braid the hair to the little ends as you secure with a thin hair tie.

Tips on how to style braids

  • During the process of adding the hair into each section, you can also add the small sections in order to achieve the curls when you release the braids.
  • In case you finger comb the sections as you braid them in, you will achieve less fly aways.
  • Ensure that you pull as tight as possible during the braiding process. To get the more curls on your hair ensure that the braids are tight enough.
  • In order to prevent the ends of your braids from looking kind of frayed, secure the hair with the use of bobby pins or a tie loop at the ends of the hair tie.

Tips on how to make the hairstyles braids curly hair

Many people find handling curly hair hard, but it doesn’t necessarily have to be. Try these easy braids styles which can be achieved in just five easy steps. Here, you can identify your curl pattern and then place the tips in order to style and also take care of your curly hair.

Step 1 - With the use of your comb and create a deep side part. Ensure that you condition and prepared the hair in order to have an easy time while braiding. Sprays the hair with the spray curl renew reactivating milk spray throughout the hair and thoroughly comb.

Step 2 - With your hand, pull out the front part of the hair. Pull the rest of the hair back and then divide it into three equal sections. It is advised to use a comb while dividing the hair to more precision instead of your fingers.
