Kate Middleton's Style Through the Years

January 26, 2023

Magenta might not be the first color that comes to mind when you think "winter wardrobe" — but it should be. Middleton proves that incorporating a bold color into your outfit is an instant mood booster. In other words, it will help to brighten your day — literally.

December 02, 2022

It only seems fitting that Princess Kate would wear green to attend an event celebrating environmentalism, namely, the Earthshot Prize. Still, the neon hue was next level, as was her dazzling diamond and emerald jewelry.

November 03, 2022

Color palettes don't always have to be loud to attract attention. Dressed in head-to-toe camel, the Duchess showed us that a muted monochrome outfit can make quite the statement.

October 13, 2022

At the 10th Anniversary Celebration of Coach Core in London, the royal aptly wore royal blue. The color adds interest and a modern twist to the classic Chanel blazer.

October 06, 2022

Visiting Belfast, Middleton paired a light blue equestrian coat with a lovely lavallière blouse. The result is very Princess Charming.

July 31, 2022

During a visit to the 1851 Trust and the Great Britain SailGP Team, the Duchess wore a nautical-themed ensemble that felt very Cape Cod (or in her case, Plymouth, England).

June 13, 2022

At the Order of The Garter service, the Duchess of Cambridge was a vision in full-on blue — from her Juliette Botterill floral leaf teardrop hat to her Alexander McQueen coatdress.

May 19, 2022

We're deeming this look Red Carpet Royalty (for obvious reasons). Middleton exuded elegance in a Roland Mouret black and white off-the-shoulder gown at the Top Gun: Maverick U.K. premiere.

March 23, 2022

Green is definitely this duchess's color. And when combined with tulle, sparkles, and the genius of designer Jenny Peckham, it gives us this head-turning number, worn during Middleton's Caribbean tour.

March 21, 2022

At a special reception in Belize, Middleton was simply show-stopping. Her sparkly pink gown by The Vampire's Wife was eveningwear at its finest.

October 19, 2021

The Duchess attended Forward Trust's "Taking Action on Addiction" campaign at BAFTA in a monochromatic all-red look that included a turtleneck sweater and matching, flowy pleated skirt. She paired the look with a neutral beige handbag and matching pointed-toe pumps.

October 17, 2021

Kate re-wore one of her iconic gowns to the first-ever Earthshot Prize event in London. The Duchess first wore the Grecian-inspired lavender gown during her and Prince William's 2011 trip to Los Angeles. This time, she paired the pleated dress with a different gold belt and wore her signature dark brown locks in a deep side part.

October 5, 2021

Kate wore a black-and-white plaid dress from Zara for a visit to the University College London's Centre for Longitudinal Studies. The Duchess paired the dress with a loosely curled hairstyle and silver snakeskin pointed-toe pumps.

September 29, 2021

For a trip to the Ulster University Magee Campus in Londonderry, Ireland, the Duchess of Cambridge chose a chic, monochromatic vibrant purple-hued suit, which she paired with a navy blue turtleneck and matching suede pumps. She completed the look with a sophisticated ponytail and a navy blue clutch.

September 28, 2021

Kate looked absolutely radiant for the No Time to Die premiere at London's Royal Albert Hall. For the debut of the new James Bond film, she wore a stunning sequin and crystal embellished dress, which included a plunging neckline, sheer skirt, and floor-grazing cape. She opted for statement earrings and an intricate updo.

March 3, 2020

Just weeks before the COVID-19 shutdown began, Kate and Prince William visited the Guinness Storehouse in Dublin, Ireland, where the Duchess even sipped on her own pint of Guinness. For the event, she went with a festive, sparkling emerald-colored midi-dress that she accessorized with matching pointed-toe suede pumps, a gold clutch, and gold drop earrings.
