31 Facts about the movie We're No Angels

Source: Paramountplus.com

We’re No Angels is a classic comedy film that has resonated with audiences since its release in 1989. Directed by Neil Jordan, the movie is filled with hilarious moments and a stellar cast that includes the likes of Robert De Niro, Sean Penn, and Demi Moore. Set on Devil’s Island, the story follows a group of escaped prisoners who disguise themselves as priests in order to avoid capture. As they try to help a struggling family, they find themselves faced with unexpected challenges and heartwarming moments. Whether you’re a fan of comedy, crime films, or simply looking for a lighthearted and entertaining movie, We’re No Angels delivers all the right ingredients for a memorable viewing experience.
