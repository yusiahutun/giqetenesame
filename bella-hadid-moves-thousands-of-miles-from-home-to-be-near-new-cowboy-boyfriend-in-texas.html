Bella Hadid 'moves thousands of miles from home' to be near new 'cowboy boyfriend' in Texas

Bella Hadid has reportedly moved to Texas full-time to be close to her ‘cowboy boyfriend’ Adan Banuelos. It marks a big move for the model, who grew up in LA.

The couple have been linked since October 2023, after they were spotted locking lips at Fort Worth stockyards. At that time, Bella had been single a few months, after splitting from former partner, Marc Kalman in Spring that year.

Bella Hadid ‘moves to Texas’ to be near ‘cowboy’ boyfriend

The 27-year-old is said to have bought a home in a rural neighborhood of Fort Worth, sources tell TMZ. It’s said the area “is close enough to urban amenities, so she doesn’t feel cut off from the world.”

Adan is a huge star in the horse-riding industry and owns a home close to where Bella has reportedly moved to. Although the couple haven’t moved in together, they’re said to spend their time between the two homes.

She’s not the only one ‘in the family’ to up sticks to be near her partner. At the end of last year, it was reported Bradley Cooper had bought a home near New Hope, Pennsylvania, close to the Hadid residence. It comes after he’s rumored to be dating Bella’s big sis, Gigi Hadid.

Bella seems to have ‘rebranded’ her Instagram to fit in with the Texas lifestyle, however, her love for horses is nothing new, as she previously told Porter Magazine she’d been ‘riding since she could walk’.

The Victoria’s Secret Angel is said to have been planning to join the Olympic equestrian team before her Lyme disease diagnosis.

The model hadn’t long come out of a two-year relationship before meeting new boyfriend

In July, it was reported Hadid had split from art director Marc Kalman, whom she had been dating for two years.

There was reported to be no bad blood between the two, as sources told Entertainment Tonight at the time, “They were very in love, but the relationship ultimately ran its course and they decided to end things.

Bella is a very nice person but struggles with the pressures of fame. She has been trying to take care of herself since their split.”

Around that time, the model was taking time off work to undergo treatment for her Lyme disease, which she was diagnosed with in 2012. Her mother Yolanda, and brother Anwar have also been diagnosed with the illness.
