Muigai wa Njoroge Savagely Responds to Man who Reminded Him of Njeri's Support: &quot;Marry Her&quot;

  • Polygamous Mugithi singer Muigai wa Njoroge and his first wife, Gladys Njeri, split and decided to co-parent
  • Njeri revealed she was hurt after her husband married a second wife, Queen Stacey, and she sank into depression
  • Netizens sympathised with Njeri, with many blasting Muigai for 'dumping' her despite building an empire with him

Muigai wa Njoroge has elicited mixed reactions on social media after his response to breaking up with his first wife, Gladys Njeri.

Why Muigai wa Njoroge and Njeri broke up

Njeri opened up about her failed marriage with the polygamous gospel singer during an interview with Kameme FM.

Asked if she was still married to Muigai, Njeri said they were no longer together. She said they tried, but it didn't work.

"Ati am I married? Not any more. We tried, I don't blame anyone, and that is the saddest thing. It never worked anger became the danger," she said. Adding: "Everything happening to me is divine. It happens for a reason. Whatever happened, I believe it was divine."

Her revelation saw many sympathise with her, including a man named James Light.

James reminded Muigai of how Njeri supported and helped him grow his music career. Then, he later repaid her by marrying a second wife.

The mother of four featured in some of her husband's videos, among them, Macaria Wi Icaririo, Ndukanduire na Marakara, Ngai Njiguuriria, Ngai Njiguuriria, and Thu Ciakwa.

"This lady supported Muigai in music so that everyone like me was looking on how we can have supportive wives like him. You invest with someone through a lot of sacrifice then another lady comes to enjoy your sweat," he wrote.

Muigai savagely responded to the man, telling him to marry Njeri now that they were no longer together.

"Now marry her so that she can compose songs and make you rich," he said.

The singer's harsh response left many in shock.

Muigai wa Njoroge and Stacey

Muigai married his second wife, Queen Stacey, 12 years ago while still married to Njeri, and she (Njeri) revealed she was bitter and became an alcoholic because she couldn't believe she had a co-wife.

Muigai and Stacey tied the knot in August 2023 in a colourful, traditional wedding.
