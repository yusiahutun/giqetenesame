Princess Sofia of Sweden volunteers at a Stockholm hospital

published on September 28, 2024
Princess Sofia of Sweden has started healthcare assistant shifts at a Swedish hospital after completing an intensive three-day programme. She was pictured wearing scrubs next to her new colleagues at Sophiahemmet University College in Stockholm, of which she is an honorary chair member. She will assist hospital staff with non-medical tasks during the pandemic, including cleaning, cooking and disinfecting equipment. The hospital has no confirmed cases of the virus to date.
