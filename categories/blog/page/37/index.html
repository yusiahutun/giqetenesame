Numerous therapies can assist in reducing stress, improving mood, and reducing pain. All of this is possible with laughter therapy, and it’s not a joke. Laughter therapy is a sort of treatment that employs humour to help people cope with stress, pain, and chronic conditions. Clowns, comic movies, literature, games, and riddles are all examples of laughter therapy exercises.
