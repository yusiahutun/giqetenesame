Vivienne Westwood has celebrated the 10-year anniversary of that famous Sex and the City wedding dress by creating a series of ready-to-wear pieces inspired by the look. The Cloud dress was first seen in the brand's Gold Label 2007-08 collection and was re-designed by the designer specifically for the character.
