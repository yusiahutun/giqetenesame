Jamie O'Hara reveals he can't wait to see his kids once he's finished work for Celebrity Big Brother

JAMIE O'Hara has revealed he can't wait to see his kids once he's finished his work with Celebrity Big Brother, calling them his "inspiration".

The football star was evicted from the house earlier this week, but has been busy doing interviews and promotional work ever since.

He's now admitted he plans to cuddle his sons for "an hour" when he finally sees them again, and has even planned a trip to a theme park.

Sharing a sweet snap of them together on Instagram, Jamie wrote: "Cannot wait to see my inspirations after my work is done for @cbb.uk going to cuddle them for an hour.

"Missed them so much, I think a trip to theme park is an idea #family #myboys #love."

Jamie has spent just over three weeks away from Archie, Harry, five, and George, two, and he told Lorraine this week he can't wait to be reunited.

Jamie said: “I missed them so much when I was in the house.

“I spoke to them last night and I’ve arranged to see them next week.”

The 30-year-old also told Lorraine he went on the show to show people the real side of him and to start a new life for his family.

He said: “I want a fresh start to build a future for me and my children.

“The situation with Danielle is difficult but it’s the past. I respect her as the mother of my kids

“Now I just want to move on with the kids. I respect her as the mother of my kids.”

Meanwhile Jamie has been celebrating his newfound freedom, and enjoyed a wild night out with his former housemates Speidi last night.

Jamie, James Jordan and Spencer Pratt joined Spurs ace Danny Rose at Libertine nightclub who looked on as the girls gyrated around each other.

The group partied at the London nightclub till the early hours after heading to Bianca Gascoigne’s strip joint The Gaslight.

In racy clips captured by former Strictly dancer James, who’s married to Ola, the star can be seen bending over as Heidi spanks her.
