50+ funniest black names that will make you laugh out loud

Black-sounding names are unique to the African-American community and are predominantly used in America. The names sound intriguing, have fascinating spellings, and bring a twist to the naming style. The funniest black names are special and carry so much meaning to their bearers.

Many theories are floating around about why some black names are spelt like they are. One popular theory is that traditional names were given to African Americans during the slavery period, but the parents could not properly spell the traditional names, thus the new spellings.

Another theory states that parents just decided to spell the names how they are spelt to add a little style. A different theory suggests that the genesis of modern black names is the civil rights movement, as blacks at the time chose to affirm and embrace the black culture.
