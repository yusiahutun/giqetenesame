Cristine Raquel Rotenberg, the woman behind Simply Nailogical, isn’t like any other YouTuber or social media star you’ve heard of. Rotenberg, who used to be a nail technician blogger, is now a beauty YouTuber who promotes her nail art and other vlogging videos.
