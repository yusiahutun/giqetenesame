Anthonys Coal Fired Pizza Helps Feeding Florida

“Right now, there is an urgent need to take care of our neighbors. It’s what we’ve always done and will continue to do.  A hot meal can provide some comfort and nourishment to families in our community and our Team is ready to help.” said, Anthony Bruno, Founder of Anthony’s Coal Fired Pizza.

Diners can order takeout by calling their local Anthony’s Coal Fired Pizza or ordering online. The delectable pies are also available for delivery through Uber Eats, DoorDash, Delivery Dudes, and GrubHub.

For more information, visit the website or follow Anthony’s on Instagram @anthonyscoalfiredpizza and on Facebook.
