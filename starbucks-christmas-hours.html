Is Starbucks Open on Christmas 2023?

Christmas is here, and as you put the finishing touches on all of your holiday plans, you're likely wondering where your Christmas coffee will come from (Merry Mint White Mocha, anyone?). Whether you're making Christmas brunch or just need a pick-me-up after getting up extra early, here's what you need to know about Starbucks' Christmas hours.

If you need a little energy boost to get through wrapping those last-minute Christmas gifts you may find yourself asking, "Is Starbucks open on Christmas 2023?"

However, a cup of coffee isn't the only thing you can enjoy at Starbucks. If you find yourself running low on hot beverages after dinner, venture down to your local store to pick up coffee beans or K-cup pods. Need a last-minute Christmas gift? Shop their large assortment of tumblers and gift cards for coffee themed gifts.

Is Starbucks Open on Christmas Day 2023?

Well, it depends! Hours vary by location, so you'll need to check before you head out the door. A representative from Starbucks told CountryLiving.com, "Store hours vary by location and stores may occasionally adjust their hours based on business and customer needs. We recommend customers look for specific store hours using the Starbucks® app or by visiting our store locator. The  Starbucks app continues to be the best way for customers to find a store, check store hours, order ahead and pay."

So there you have it. Whether you're looking for a plain cup of coffee to keep you awake, or are craving a signature seasonal beverage, you're just a few clicks away from knowing that your local Starbucks—or one nearby—will be open.
