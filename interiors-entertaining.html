Camp David In Industry City

For the freelancer looking to escape a rogue coffee shop lifestyle, to the stylish startup looking for a space to spearhead humble beginnings, the newly opened Camp David in Brooklyn’s Industry City is a chic answer to your workspace woes.

Courtesy Camp David

The expertly tailored workspace offers various types of memberships depending on your needs, whether you’re simply looking for a more open place to work amongst young professionals at a custom crafted desk, or want your own acoustically sound private office a few floors up.

Courtesy Camp David

“The creative fervor and sense of possibility in this neighborhood is palpable,” says co-founder Mazdack Rassi. “Here, there is just so much space—space to think, space to spread out, space to create and collaborate, space to evolve and grow.”

Courtesy Camp David

While there are already office spaces that exist within the six million square feet of Industry City, Camp David is unique in that its one of the first structures to allow for a month-to-month membership versus a yearly lease. This also means that everything already comes furnished, and all you have to do is show up with your creative flow brimming.

Courtesy Camp David

However, “furnished” may be an understatement with Camp David, for there are some pretty nice amenities that come along with your membership. For example, there’s a bar that serves up cappuccinos and avocado toast by day, and transforms into a stylish bar setting by night. There’s also a full-time concierge service where members can truly get the high level of personal service they need.

Courtesy Camp David

“From the beginning, our intention was to create something that didn’t already exist within the co-working sphere,” reveals Madeline Rudin, the Managing Director of Camp David. “Everything from the acoustics to the lighting were carefully considered and the attention to detail was something that came up in every meeting surrounding the project.”

Courtesy Camp David

An attention to detail is exactly what was achieved with Camp David, forgoing the transient feeling that often comes with co-working spaces. Instead, you'll find beautiful, light-filled offices that make you feel right at home, boasting anything but a temporary workplace.

Courtesy Camp David

“We really wanted businesses to come and grow within the space versus out of the space,” Rudin expresses in regards to the "motto" of the project.

It’s safe to say that Camp David has done just this with its gorgeous wood desks, marble countertops and sleek modern art, fashioning the chicest workspace we’ve ever seen.

Courtesy Camp David

Private office membership begins at $1,500 per month; assigned desk membership begins at $700 per month; work lounge membership begins $1,200 per year. For further membership details, visit thecampdavid.com.
