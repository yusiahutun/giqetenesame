More about Hattie Boydle

Hattie Boydle is a professional fitness model and 2016 World Beauty Fitness and Fashion (WBFF) World Champion. In addition, an advocate for body positivity, motivational speaker, and entrepreneur.

She is the founder of the Sports Model Project, a prominent online fitness and coaching program. She is on a quest to motivate women to achieve their full potential. Whether through weight training, healthy living, competing on stage, or simply being the best version of themselves.

Sports Model Project Purpose

Hattie Boydle’s purpose with the Sports Model Project is to empower women to love their bodies and offer them the support structure they need to attain their health and fitness goals conscientiously.

She has worked with hundreds of women who have positively improved their lives through certified workouts, health guidance, and coaching techniques. While she is now the happiest, she has ever been, physically, intellectually, and spiritually. However, her road has not been without hardships.

The hardships she overcame

Hattie, a fitness model, has always been unafraid to venture outside of her comfort zone and challenge herself. Her desire to compete and perform began at four when her parents enrolled her in gymnastics.

Hattie quickly rose to the ranks of the top gymnast, training at the AIS and winning numerous national-level events.

However, as she grew older, she experienced certain difficulties. She was bullied in primary school, and by the time she was finally stable and happy in high school, one of her best friends died in a horrible accident.

From there, she descended into deep despair and became obsessed with having complete control over her life. Unfortunately, this resulted in an unhealthy relationship with food, and she was forced to fight anorexia for a long time, which got exhausting.

Hattie Boydle’s motivation

Hattie’s life changed when she ended up in the emergency room and was admitted to a three-month in-patient hospital stay. She’ll never forget the agony of being surrounded by so many sick girls who appeared helpless.

Something unfolded, and she realized that things needed to change. Hattie realized she had to be someone who could teach these ladies how to care for themselves healthily. She wished for women to achieve self-acceptance and to be at ease in their skin.

Hattie Boydle Now

Hattie is now delighted to say that she has discovered her happy medium between being fit, healthy, and strong. Furthermore, She has dedicated herself to learning and surrounding herself with individuals who want to see her thrive throughout the years.

Hattie has accumulated a plethora of skills in both training and diet. Therefore, she is thrilled to share with the public through the Sports Model Project.
