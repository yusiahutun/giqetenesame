'I want to be 100% plastic': Human Ken doll risks blindness to have his 'Julia Roberts' forehead vei

Justin Jedlica has already had 190 cosmetic procedures in his bid to transform himself into a human Ken doll.

And now, the self-proclaimed plastic surgery addict has gone under the knife once again, this time removing what he calls 'Julia Roberts' veins' in his forehead, marking his most dangerous procedure ever.

The 34-year-old Botched star, from New York, also underwent a four-piece back enhancement surgery using implants he handcrafted himself.
