Lolo Jones puts on a VERY leggy display in racy transparent black mini dress at 2023 ESPY Awards

Lolo Jones had all eyes on her as she hit the red carpet at the 2023 ESPY Awards on Wednesday, held at the iconic Dolby Theatre in Hollywood, California.

The Olympic star, 40, wowed in a racy black mini dress with a transparent front that showed off her sculpted abs at sports' biggest night.

The sensational dress featured a chunky silver choker, which the athlete paired with dangling silver star earrings.

The famous hurdler and bobsledder showed off her incredibly toned legs and added extra height to the look with a pair of silver Giuseppe Zanotti stilettos. 

Other stars and athletes at the event included Tiffany Haddish, Olympian Shawn Johnson and WWE superstars Brie and Nikki Bella.

The ESPY Awards celebrates 'excellence in sports performance', but also gives those usually drenched in sweat on a field or court the chance to dress up and show off their style. 

The sports star, originally from Iowa - who was already breaking track records by the time she graduated college - has become one of the most accomplished hurdlers in the world.

Lolo looked to be in good spirits after suffering a scary stalking incident earlier this year. 

Lolo revealed she has had three different male stalkers in the past year, with one turning up on her front door and another breaking into the Olympic training center.

In a somber Instagram post this January, the Olympic gold medalist appeared to look upset in a black hooded jacket as she said the incidents had 'altered my life' and jeopardized the safety of her friends and herself.

Jones, who has famously chosen to remain a virgin, also uploaded a photo from her front door camera appearing to show a stranger at her home who insisted to cops she had invited him to live with her.
