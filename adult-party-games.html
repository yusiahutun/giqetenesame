Charades Party

Image Source//Getty Images

Charades has been a crowd-pleaser since the 18th century. Pick up a deck of charades cards or write ideas on slips of paper ahead of time. Split up into teams, and get to guessing! One person pantomimes clues without talking or pointing to objects in the room. Each correct answer gets a point, and the team with the most points wins.
