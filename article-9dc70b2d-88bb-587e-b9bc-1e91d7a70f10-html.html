South Jones homecoming court | Education

South Jones High School will kick off homecoming festivities with a downtown Ellisville parade on Thursday, Sept. 14, at 6:30 p.m. The homecoming court will be presented at 9:30 a.m., in the school gymnasium on Sept. 15 and again at 6:15 p.m., on the football field at The Reservation before the South Jones game vs. Bay High. Front row, from left, Timber Prince, student body maid; Endia Holliday, Miss South Jones; Darby Cooley, homecoming queen; Emma Kate Prince, Miss South Jones Junior High; and Allison Deloach, student body maid. Middle row, Keosha Miranda, junior maid; Skyla Langley, senior maid; Cameron Sanford, football maid; Callie Shows, senior maid; Whitney Kirksey, football maid; Cambry Butler, senior maid; and Anna Brownlee, junior maid. Back row, Phallyn Clark, eighth-grade maid; Autumn Henderson, freshman maid; Madison Jones, sophomore maid; Kaitlyn Kirksey, sophomore maid; Sabreya McDonald, freshman maid; and Abby Harper, eighth-grade maid.
