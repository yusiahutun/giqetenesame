Is Eric Stonestreet Gay?

The comedian and actor Eric has played on the famous gay couple on the TV with Jesse Tyler Ferguson (Mitchell Pritchett) as Cameron Tucker. Even though he played as gay on the television, he is straight in real life while his partner Tyler is gay. Eric even said to Oprah.com, “I say I’m ‘openly straight. I never thought this would be the character that people would know me for, but it’s just an honor to play him.”

They met each other for coffee on Valentine’s Day to discuss their roles after they found out they are going to play as a gay couple; his partner Jesse said about him, 

“Eric is straight. I call him gay-for-pay. He is so game for this character. We just clicked. We hang out a lot. Ironically, after he got the role, we decided to meet up for coffee. We’re having coffee in the middle of the afternoon – and we realize that it’s Valentine’s Day! Just two guys, hangin’ out, having coffee. No big deal.”

He was asked the question,” How difficult is that to play as someone who isn’t gay?” where he replied, 

“It’s not that much of a deal because I’m an actor and play all kinds of roles. The fact Cam is gay doesn’t have much to do with anything. I played bad guys on TV before, other guys before, so it’s a character I get to play and never really thought too much about it.”

It's clear that he was gay in his role only but not in his real life.
