Why is Kyle Kuzma famous?

Michigan-born Kuzma is 28 and plays as a forward for the Washington Wizards in the NBA, per his profile on the league’s website. He was previously a rookie player with the Los Angeles Lakers in 2017, and was with the Brooklyn Nets before that, per Sports Illustrated.“It’s surreal,” Kuzma told Bleacher Report in 2017. “The Lakers are the premier franchise in all of basketball. It’s the biggest brand in basketball. It’s a really surreal feeling to wake up every day, put on my Lakers gear and see Magic in there and play for Luke Walton.”

Does Kyle Kuzma have a degree?

How much money has Kyle Kuzma made?

Per Celebrity Net Worth, Kuzma has amassed a fortune of around US$13 million thanks to his lucrative NBA career. He’s also signed various endorsement deals with labels including Puma and GOAT, per Sportskeeda.

Seven years into his NBA career, and after a profitable transfer to the Washington Wizards that snagged him a US$102 million contract over four years according to CBS Sports, Kuzma’s financial standing has soared spectacularly, with a bank balance that’s grown by leaps and (re) bounds.

How did Kyle Kuzma meet Winnie Harlow?

Kuzma met Winnie Harlow after connecting via Instagram.

Harlow opened up about their first interaction in an appearance on The Ellen DeGeneres Show: “At the top of quarantine, he DMed me again … He shot his shot. He slid in the DMs. That was cute. I was like, ‘Oh hey, how’s quarantine been?’”

Per People, they started talking every day after that and ended up meeting in person. “After a month of being on FaceTime for 24 hours every single day for a month, he was like, ‘You gotta come to LA,’” Harlow said on the show. “And I’ve been in LA ever since.”

In an interview with People, Kuzma spoke about his admiration for the supermodel. “We always bounce ideas off each other,” he shared in 2022, “[Winnie] has a lot of style, too, and that’s one of the things that’s connecting me to her and you know we love fashion, we love art, we love clothing.”
