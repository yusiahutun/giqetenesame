Alex Pereira's bio

Alex was born on July 7, 1987, which means he is 35 years old as of 2022. His birthplace is Sao Bernardo do Campo, in the State of Sao Paulo, Brazil. Pereira spent most of his early childhood years in Brazil. He grew up alongside his sibling Aline Pereira, a professional kickboxer.

Alex has kept details about his parents under wraps. He dropped out of middle school and started working at a fire shop when he was a teenager. Alex became an alcoholic, and he enrolled in kickboxing classes to get rid of the addictive behaviour.

Career

Alex debuted his kickboxing career in 2009. However, his breakthrough came in March 2013 when he won the middleweight contender tournament and defeated Dustin Jacoby in the first round. After that, he took part in various Glory middleweight tournaments.

Pereira won the Glory middleweight title and defended it a record four times. He also won the light heavyweight title at the Glory 77 event. Alex Pereira's kickboxing record was quite impressive as he became the first fighter to hold two belts at the same time simultaneously. In 2015, he transitioned from kickboxing to Mixed Martial Arts (MMA).

He signed a contract with Legacy Fighting Alliance in October 2020 and won his debut match against Thomas Powell. The most significant break in his MMA career came in 2021 when he signed with the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). He made his debut against Andreas Michallidis and won the fight via technical knockout.

Alex Pereira's UFC debut match win earned him the Performance of the Night award. He defeated Bruno Silva and Sean Strickland in subsequent UFC matches. He has already made it to the top of the UFC rankings table. Currently, he ranks 5th on the list of the best MMA middleweight fighters.
