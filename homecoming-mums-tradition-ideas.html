Everything to Know About Homecoming Mums

1

The Homecoming Mum's Humble Beginnings

H. Armstrong Roberts/ClassicStock//Getty Images

An early picture of a couple in the 1960s showcases the early tradition of mums as football game accessories. (Note the reasonable size and relatively understated ribbons!)

2

State-Shaped Homecoming Mum

Fast forward 50 years, and you have a back-breaking, Texas-shaped mum that boasts multiple teddy bears, ribbons galore, and a glitzy "Senior" denotation.

3

How to Make Your Own Homecoming Mum

It's not quite a Texas-sized masterpiece, but here's a quick tutorial on how to make a mum. If you're convinced that bigger is always better, you should know that the large-scale mums are going to use up hundreds of yards of ribbon and about 14 hours of your life.

Customized Homecoming Mums

Light It Up
