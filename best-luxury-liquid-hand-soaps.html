Luxury hand wash | 18+ best liquid hand soaps to invest in now

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Dior Lucky Liquid Soap

Dior Lucky Liquid Soap

Credit: Dior

A seriously chic move, Dior's Lucky hand soap boasts a luxurious lather and smells as fresh as a bouquet of lilies. It's such a joy to use that you'll never rush your way through washing your hands again.

Pelegrims Hand Cleanser

Pelegrims Hand Cleanser

Now 42% Off

Credit: Humanery

Small-batch beauty brand Pelegrims sources its raw ingredients from the bottom of the winemaker's barrel. Based in Kent, the independent operation buys waste product from Westwell Wines, using it to enrich its 'low-intervention' formulas.

A great introduction to the brand, the Hand Cleanser contains English ortega grape extract alongside crushed walnut shells for satisfying grit. It smells predictably delicious, and will look chic on the side of any sink.

Space NK Rewild Hand Wash

Space NK Rewild Hand Wash

Credit: Space NK

Space NK's own hand and body line was designed to fill a gap in the retailer's shelves, offering luxe scents and skin-kind formulas with a price tag that means you don't feel compelled to 'save for best'.

The Rewild hand soap is our top pick: sweet almond and coconut oils leave hands feeling nourished, while the fragrant black pepper and juniper blend is a breath of fresh air.

The icing on the cake? The bottles can be refilled in flagship stores across the country – and there's a matching hand lotion to place alongside.

Diptyque Exfoliating Hand Soap

Diptyque Exfoliating Hand Soap

Credit: Space NK

Clearly the most beautiful bathroom addition, Diptyque's engraved glass soap dispenser is designed to be treasured: it can be kept topped up using the brand's low-waste (and pleasingly priced) refill pouches.

Not just an extremely chic choice, the formula inside is a treat too: tiny olive-stone beads gently exfoliate, while the green, floral scent leaves a refreshing trail.

Loewe Liqcuorice Liquid Soap

Loewe Liqcuorice Liquid Soap

Credit: Selfridges

Big Fashion is coming for the homeware market, but few have managed to make the humble hand soap as covetable as Loewe. These chic (and heavy) glass bottles house a gentle, conditioning soap in the brand's much-loved greenhouse-inspired scents.

Ortigia Florio Liquid Soap in Glass Bottle

Ortigia Florio Liquid Soap in Glass Bottle

Ortigia prides itself in showcasing the best of Sicily's opulent aesthetics, and this sophisticated liquid soap is no exception: as ornamental as it is functional, it's a real work of art.

The formula is infused with the brand's Florio scent: a refreshing blend of jasmine and wild iris.

Commune Seymour Hand Wash

Commune Seymour Hand Wash

Credit: Harrods

Independent UK-based brand Commune produces thoughtful products that marry sustainable practice with total luxury. The brand's small-batch hand wash is made with gentle cleansing ingredients, moisturising olive oil, and a blend of invigorating essential oils. The bottle is made from infinitely recyclable aluminium.

Aesop Resurrection Aromatique Hand Wash

Aesop Resurrection Aromatique Hand Wash

Credit: Space NK

If Aesop's hand soap looks familiar, it's because it features in the bathroom of many a buzzy bar and restaurant across the country. (Rumour has it, the brand personally approves every placement.)

Not just a very good-looking bottle, this refreshing soap is fragranced with rosemary and lavender essential oils: a truly crowd-pleasing scent.

Guerlain Herbes Troublantes Hand Soap

Guerlain Herbes Troublantes Hand Soap

Credit: Guerlain

As crisp and refreshing as the matching cologne, Guerlain's liquid soap is a seriously refined option. The faceted glass bottle is the weightiest of the bunch, and the lather is fine and frothy. Double the trail with the matching hand lotion.

Penhaligon's The Favourite Body & Hand Wash

Penhaligon's The Favourite Body & Hand Wash

Credit: Penhaligon's

Inspired by Sarah Churchill, Duchess of Marlborough, Penhaligon's delicately scented hand soap is nothing short of decadent. Luxe from skin to scent, this nurturing olfactive rinse not only duplicates the same golden mimosa and soft musk aromas of the matching perfume, it also contains a trifecta of hydrating ingredients: vitamin E, glycerin and panthenol.

Le Labo Hinoki Hand Soap

Le Labo Hinoki Hand Soap

Credit: Cult Beauty

Le Labo's hand wash may look understated to the untrained eye, but those in the know will instantly recognise the cream and black bottle. Rich and creamy, this hydrating wash will leave hands soft and nourished, while the woody, incense-spiked Hinoki fragrance is sure to please.

Fresh Rose Morning Body & Hand Wash

Fresh Rose Morning Body & Hand Wash

Credit: Space NK

Musk and peach bring a soft, fuzzy-edged new feel to Fresh's classic rose fragrance this season.

Wildsmith Skin Hand and Body Wash

Wildsmith Skin Hand and Body Wash

Now 25% Off

Credit: Humanery

Many of the plant extracts in Wildsmith Skin's creations are grown on the grounds of Heckfield Place – the birthplace of the brand. Combining local sourcing with potent aromatherapy properties – expect a calming blend of chamomile and woods – this hand wash encapsulates modern luxury.

Byredo Rose Hand Wash

Byredo Rose Hand Wash

Credit: Space NK

As chic as the brand's much-Instagrammed perfume bottles, this weighty liquid hand soap is a very sleek choice. The fragrance blends rose with raspberry and musk, while the formula is gentle and nourishing. Team it with the matching hand cream for a lasting scent.

Ouai Hand Wash Dean Street

Ouai Hand Wash Dean Street

Credit: Cult Beauty

Trust Ouai to breathe new life into the hand-wash category. Part of the brand's new bodycare line, this minimalist bottle contains a rich, low-foaming liquid soap that leaves hands feeling refreshed and replenished. The meadow-fresh Dean Street fragrance is a fan favourite for good reason, and the biodegradable exfoliating particles only enhance the spa-like luxury.

The bottle is recyclable – but why throw it away when you can keep it topped up with the brand's eco refills?

Jo Malone London Lime Basil & Mandarin Body & Hand Wash

Jo Malone London Lime Basil & Mandarin Body & Hand Wash

Jo Malone London is undoubtedly the most classic choice, and the timeless Lime, Basil & Mandarin remains our favourite scent year-round. Zesty and sharp, it refreshes hands and the senses.

Malin + Goetz Rum Hand + Body Wash

Malin + Goetz Rum Hand + Body Wash

Boasting Malin + Goetz''s cult-favourite dark rum scent, this indulgent wash will linger on your skin beyond the bubbles and rinse. The brown-sugar sweetness is tempered by a warming woody base, creating a scent so good you'll want to slather it on head to toe (good thing there's a matching Perfume Oil, then.)
