Michael Buble responds to corn on the cob meme

Michael Bublé has posted a funny response after a photo of him eating a corn on the cob went viral. The picture, taken at Disneyland last week, shows the singer holding the cob upright, like an ice cream, as he prepares to take a big bite from the top – and fans quickly started to joke about his unusual eating technique.Responding to the funny image, which has been meme-ified a number of times, the Canadian star wrote: "This is vegetable harassment!!! What I do with my Cobb is nobody's business. #mesocorny #paparazzi #shuckme."

Michael had a hilarious response to memes of his corn on the cob photo

It's no wonder Michael is in such high spirits. He and his wife Luisana Lopilato have been enjoying their precious first few months with second son Elias, who was born on 22 January. The couple are also parents to two-year-old Noah, and are clearly totally besotted with their "little men".Just last month Argentine model Luisana shared a photograph of herself cuddled up with her little boys. "How can I explain this photo? I can't!" the 28-year-old captioned the image. "I melt and I melt every day more because of these little men."

Michael and Luisana are proud parents to two little boys

Michael, meanwhile, has previously said that becoming a father again is "as good as it gets". The proud dad announced the birth of his second child by sharing a family photo from the hospital, writing: "And then there were 4. Introducing Noah's little brother Elias Buble #family #blessed #brothers."

The singer recently shared an adorable photo of two-year-old Noah

Michael, 40, also described firstborn Noah as his "everything" in another touching post from a recent holiday to Hawaii. Alongside an image showing Michael with the toddler sat on his shoulders, the singer wrote: "I hate posting just anything, so instead, I'm posting my everything. We're on the best vacation ever. Lots of sunshine with my sunshine. #family #vacation #meandmyshadow."
