Mother's Day Spa Specials 2023

This Mother's Day, what better way to thank that special mother figure in your life than with a much-needed spa day. We know that years of dealing with our mood swings, coming to our sports games (even when we were just bench warmers), and grappling with teen angst can't be summed up into one day of the year, but the least we can do is give her some well-deserved TLC. Whether she likes breakfast in bed or prefers a gift that's meaningful, the experience of pure relaxation is a love language we all share. We rounded up some awesome spa specials and deals to help you celebrate that special mother figure in your life without breaking the bank. With these specials, you may even want to make it an appointment for two *wink wink*. Before booking, we suggest calling your local location ahead of time to determine their hours of operation and if they're participating in the Mother's Day special. Also, if you don't happen to see a spa near you on the list, grab a gift card from your local spa because any massage or facial is better than none at all.

Brighten up mom's day with the gift of a relaxing facial. This year, Hand and Stone is giving $20 off their new one-hour facial gift card and $10 off their massage gift card. Might as well get both, right?

Mom always has your back so now it's time to take a load off of hers. LaVida is offering a free $25 bonus card when you purchase $100 in gift cards before 6/19.

Celebrate the face that always had the warmest smile for you at Massage Envy. They're offering a buy one, get the second free special for 60-minute customized facial sessions (must be purchased by May 8).

Give your mom the gift of thanks with Massage Heights' "Mother's Happiness" Massage offer which includes a 90-minute massage, hot stone therapy, and a limited-time offer of a margarita foot scrub. Plus, they're offering a $25 promo card with the purchase of a "Mother's Happiness" Gift Card.

Give mom a moment of relaxation with a 90-minute massage at Massage Luxe. This Mother's Day, they're giving you a massage with the addition of two add-ons or VIP facial for only $99.
