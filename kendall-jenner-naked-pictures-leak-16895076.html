Kendall Jenner naked pics leak online after images are stolen in online attack

The reality TV beauty has posed in her birthday suit for an artistic coffee table book by photographer Russell James.

The images were initially shot for his forthcoming book Angels – however, it is not clear if they had been selected to make the final cut.

US site TMZ adds the images were "stolen from Russell and released without permission".

Neither Kendall nor Russell have commented on the pictures finding their way online.

In the images, Kim Kardashian's sister can be seen in a range of locations naked, including at the beach, up a tree and riding a horse.

Chatting about the project before the leak, the photographer told Vogue: "I started this project in 2014 and was collaborating with each and every person to find out exactly how they wanted to be portrayed, so we critiqued each shot and talked about it together."

Speaking at an exhibition of the art in New York, he added: "So this was built on a trust between me and the model, but it was also built on this idea that we don’t want to stop this beauty."
