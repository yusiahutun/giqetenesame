Pippa Middleton Wears a White Bikini on a Family Trip to St. Barts

Pippa Middleton is starting off 2019 in vacation mode. The British socialite (and younger sister of Kate Middleton) was seen soaking up the sun with family members in St. Barts, an island in the French Caribbean, according to People. She was accompanied by her husband James Matthews, in-laws Spencer Matthews and Vogue Williams, brother James Middleton, and his rumored girlfriend. Unfortunately, Kate and Prince William were missing in action.

For the family vacay, the stylish new mom opted for a white bikini—by British label Biondi London—with a decorative blue crochet trim. In one shot, Middleton was also seen clutching a pair of goggles while strolling on the shore.

MEGA

One important family member missing from the beach outing was Pippa and James' new baby, Arthur. The baby boy, the couple's first child, was born on October 15. Although the two-month-old didn't tag along for the beach trip, he reportedly did travel with the family to St. Barts. Photos published on Mirror show Pippa showing her baby to father-in-law David Matthews ahead of the new year.

It's unclear how Pippa, James, and Arthur spent Christmas, but in early December, the first-time parents were seen attending a caroling service for the Henry van Straubenzee Memorial Fund. Prince Harry and Meghan Markle were also in attendance.

Pippa and James weren't present when the extended royal family attended church service on Christmas Day. However, those in attendance included the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge, the Duke and Duchess of Sussex, Prince Charles, the Queen, and more royal relatives.
