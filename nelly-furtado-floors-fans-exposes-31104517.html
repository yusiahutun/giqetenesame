Nelly Furtado floors fans as she exposes ageless curves in nude illusion SKIMS set

Maneater songstress Nelly Furtado looked ageless as ever as she slipped into nude illusion underwear to take part in Kim Kardashian’s latest SKIMS campaign. Nelly looked incredible in a plunging balcony-style bra and matching high-waisted sheer shapewear, with her matching undies visible underneath.

With her slender legs on full display, Nelly donned a pair of sheer tights and point-toe black stiletto heels for the shoot, leaning back against a white cube as she put her lithe frame front and centre.

The songstress let her sleek brunette hair tumble in loose waves past her shoulders and embraced a bronzed make-up look for the shoot. Pairing a golden brown metallic eyeshadow with a pale, nude lip, Nelly’s highlighter was sharp enough to cut as she went jewellery-free for the event.
