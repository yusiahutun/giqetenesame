Top 10 most popular girls games you should play in 2021

Traditionally, people have equated gaming as a principal activity for males. It is one reason why most games you find on any gaming platform are built for the male gender in mind. As a result, many girls looking to play a video game can find it challenging to find one that suits their personalities. But in recent years, many female gamers have risen to compete with boys. However, most of them prefer playing specific games. So, which are the most popular girls games?

Nowadays, it is not uncommon to find developers developing videos games for the female gender. Many are creating games for girls because they consider it an underserved market. Of course, you will also find many more developers developing games for both genders. But some girls today prefer playing specific types of games, whether designed for boys or girls.

10 most popular girls games

What game has the most female players? Fortnite and Pokemon Go are two of the most played games by girls. That is not a surprise because Fortnite has over 350 million players worldwide, and Pokemon Go has over 160 million players. Here is a list of ten of the most popular video games for females.

1. Animal Crossing

Animal Crossing is one of the best video games for girls, especially between 19 and 24. The game is popular among ladies because it incentivises creativity by allowing players to decorate and modify their island to their liking. Furthermore, the game is about friendship, community, and family, three factors that appeal to the female gender.

Unlike many other games, it provides players with an escapist dreamland that is at once comforting, relaxing, and heart-warming. In addition, although many girls love playing the game, the face options in Animal Crossing for a player character are gender-neutral, even if one has no possibility of choosing between being a boy or a girl.

2. Assassin’s Creed Syndicate

If you are familiar with the Assassin’s Creed video game series, you know by now that most people that buy and play the game are male. That has been the case with previous releases but not with Assassin’s Creed Syndicate. It is an action-adventure video game that lets players take turns playing as both a male and female protagonist.

It is also the first game in the series to introduce a female character. The female factor is one of the reasons the game is popular with girls. Furthermore, women play the game because it is fun, breezy, well-written, and well-paced.

3. Bejewelled

Women are more likely going to play puzzle games than sports games. That is why Bejewelled has become one of the best games for girl gamers because of the many puzzles one must solve. Generally, Bejewelled is a poster child for a genre of casual games that revolve around shifting coloured blocks to match three or more of them in a line.

Unlike many traditional video games, Bejewelled does not have the option for a gamer to set the difficulty level. Instead, the game is all about how far you can go when playing it. The laid back factor makes the game resonate with women.

4. Candy Crush Saga

A publication in 2015 by The Conversation showed that 62% of people that play Candy Crush Saga are women. One of the reasons women find the game attractive is because it is free and available on mobile devices. Furthermore, the game is simple to learn and addictive once a person gets started.

Furthermore, the structure of the game is different and unique compared to the traditional match-three games. For example, Candy Crush Saga will allow you to win early on. But then the game gets harder and harder, making you want to win. Although losing is an option, it is painful for most people.

5. Dragon Age: Inquisition

Dragon Age: Inquisition is one of the most popular PC games for women. Around 48% of gamers that play the game are women. One reason for its popularity among women is because one can choose a female character to play with from the beginning to the end.

6. Farm Heroes Saga

Which games do girls play the most? Farm Heroes Saga. It is another video game available on mobile devices and is from the makers of Candy Crush Saga. The playing experience is almost similar to Candy Crush Saga. That explains why the game is more popular with girls.

As a puzzle game, it involves matching fruits and vegetables to earn points. However, one thing that makes the game unique from puzzle games is its objectives. A player must fulfil a specific goal before they can proceed to the next level.

7. Minecraft

Minecraft is a game of all ages. People above ten years can play and enjoy it. Although it is also a game of all genders, about 32% of Minecraft players are women. Many girls prefer playing Minecraft because it has a creative streak that allows them to mould their gameplay in any way they choose.

8. Stardew Valley

Stardew Valley is one of the best games for teenage girls. Most females play the game because it is more casual and laid back. Furthermore, girls can do many things when playing the game.

For example, they can create the farm of their dreams by turning their overgrown fields into lively and bountiful farms. Also, they can learn to live off the land, raise animals, and even go fishing. The combination of addicting gameplay and excellent characters makes the game worth playing.

9. Star Wars: The Old Republic

Star Wars: The Old Republic is a free-to-play massively multiplayer online role-playing game. It attracts players across genders and ages because of its eight fully voiced stories. The game also gives players many playing choices, with each option made changing how their story unfolds.

10. The Sims

The Sims is a series of life simulation video games that enjoys a higher proportion of girls that play the game.
