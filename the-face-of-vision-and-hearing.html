The FACE of Vision and Hearing

Lydia Schmidt, Schmidt’s Optical and Hearing

Schmidt’s Optical and Hearing, with a legacy in selection and innovation, has redefined the art of enhancing vision and hearing, making it more accessible and personalized than ever.

Owner and second-generation optician, Lydia Schmidt is at its heart. She is a visionary entrepreneur who leads in providing world-class services. Schmidt’s commitment to personal professional service from start to finish is the leading force behind the Schmidt’s team of highly trained professionals.

What sets Schmidt’s Optical and Hearing apart is its unparalleled selection of eyewear, catering to every budget without compromising on style or quality. Frames featuring exclusive luxury in Martin County to economy less than any box store, customers can find their perfect match effortlessly. Schmidt’s full on-site lab further streamlines the process, ensuring rapid one-hour service and precise customization.

In addition, Schmidt’s extends its expertise to hearing care, addressing a crucial aspect of overall well-being. By offering the six leading manufacturers in stock, the practice can meet all hearing needs.

In a world where quality and convenience often collide, Schmidt’s emerges as the model of excellence. Through Lydia Schmidt’s leadership, the company’s commitment to premium eyewear and advanced hearing solutions stands as a testament to the remarkable synergy between innovation, accessibility, and genuine care.

Schmidt’s Optical and Hearing is celebrating its 45th year in Martin County.
