The King Power Royal Charity Polo Day took place earlier today, and both Prince William and Prince Harry participated in the sporting event. And while the brothers battled it out, their wives and children cheered them on from the sidelines.

Meghan Markle and her son, Archie, put in an adorable appearance at the event, just days after the baby boy's Archie's christening. The mother-son duo stayed close to Kate Middleton and her children, making it a real family day out.

But it was Prince Louis's cool shades that really stole the show, with the little prince donning the oversized sunglasses as his mom crouched close by. As @MiddletonMaven points out, Louis appears to have swiped Kate's shades at the event, which explains why they're a little big for him:

The charity polo match is being held in honor of Vichai Srivaddhanaprabha, the owner of Leicester City soccer club who sadly died in a helicopter crash in October 2018. The Duke and Duchess of Cambridge also visited Leicester in November 2018 to pay tribute to Srivaddhanaprabha.
