Portia de Rossi, 50, debuts wavy new hairstyle as she declares 'I'm bringing the perm back!' and sha

Portia de Rossi is over the moon about her curly new permed look.

The 50-year-old actress - who recently bought a new home - took to Instagram this week to share her fresh hairstyle with her 1.4 million followers.

Posting a photo with gray rods in her blonde hair and a black smock covering her, she declared, 'THIS IS A PERM, PEOPLE!!'

The star, who is married to Ellen DeGeneres, lauded company Curl Cult as she credited hairstylist Janine Jarman.

Along with a carousel of images showing off her hair transformation, she gushed, 'I am so impressed with Janine Jarman that I had to post the ugliest pic of myself getting a perm just to prove to you what a genius this woman is!'

Portia's note continued, '@curlcult has created a patent pending formula which makes beach waves a reality.

'It’s vegan, my hair feels healthy, and no more hot tools. I love it. I air dry my hair and I always have loose waves no matter how humid it is outside.'

Then she noted, 'Btw—I am not getting paid for this. I just had to let everyone know that I’m bringing the perm back.'

de Rossi included a set of snapshots showing off her newly textured hair from different angles.

Styled in an undefined center part, her shoulder-length golden locks appeared lustrous and bouncy.

The post snagged over 44,000 likes from Portia's fans, as well as a host of comments, including one from Courteney Cox, who wrote, 'Gorgeous!'

In two makeup-free selfies, the Australian-born entertainer donned a white tank top as she captured her image.

In another two snaps, she pulled her hair into a low bun and gave viewers a closer look.

'PS…I’m not paid to do this! I just really want to show all the folks who thought I was crazy for wanting a perm! You know who you are…' she finished the caption.
