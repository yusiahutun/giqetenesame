Christopher Lim Legaspi (born January 1, 1973), popularly known as Kier Legaspi, is a Filipino actor known for his villain roles in the 90s. He is the younger brother of fellow actors Zoren Legaspi and Brando Legaspi (whose real name is "Kier Lim Legaspi", a name he borrowed and used on-screen) and is also a son of Lito Legaspi. He has one daughter with actress Marjorie Barretto named Daniella (Dani).
