What Is Depth Psychology?

published on June 22, 2024
The practice and study of the science of the unconscious, which incorporates both psychoanalysis and psychology, is alluded to as depth psychology (from the German term Tiefenpsychologie). Eugen Bleuler first used the term “depth psychology” to portray psychoanalytic strategies of treatment and study that consider the unconscious. The examination of the unconscious and its relationship to the conscious mind was the start of depth psychology.
