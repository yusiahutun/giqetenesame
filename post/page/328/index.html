ObituaryOscar ShumskyAmerican violinist held in awe for his technical virtuosity, lyrical playing and musical memoryThe American violinist Oscar Shumsky, who has died aged 83, was regarded by many musicians as one of the greatest 20th-century exponents of his instrument. His intensely communicative yet refined playing combined the grand romantic tradition of Russian-Jewish violinists with the same dash of eloquence that characterised the playing of his idol, Fritz Kreisler. His immaculate technical command was legendary, and his lyrical playing was distinguished by a luscious warmth and highly expressive intonation. Read More...
