Crews pull man from Mississippi River after he falls off barge

published on July 08, 2024
A water rescue happened late Sunday afternoon after a man fell off a barge near the Grain Processing Center at 1600 Oregon Street, Muscatine. Around 3:22 p.m., the Muscatine County Joint Communications Center (MUSCOM) was notified that an individual had fallen off a barge and was unable to climb out of the river. Muscatine Fire Department launched a rescue boat with two firefighters from the new boat launch on the upriver side of Riverside Park while other on-duty personnel went to the levee at the Grain Processing Center facility to assist, a news release says.
