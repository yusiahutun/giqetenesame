Self Concept: Definition, Factors and Elements

published on March 23, 2024
Our understanding of who we are, comprising all our ideas and emotions regarding our physical, psychological, and social selves, is known as our self-concept. Self-concept also encompasses our perception of our unique qualities, capacities, and behaviours. Early infancy and adolescence are when our self-concept grows the fastest, yet it is a dynamic idea that evolves as we gain more self-awareness.
