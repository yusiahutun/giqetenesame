This Hair Treatment For Thinning Hair is a Favorite of Jennifer Lopez

Thinning hair can feel like a constant battle as you tease and snip your tresses to help add volume. No matter what you do, sometimes it feels like you’re finding more hair on surfaces than on your actual head. But you’re not alone, even A-list celebrities like Jennifer Lopez struggle to thicken their hair. Recently, Lopez shared her secret hair treatment to preventing hair loss — and it’s only $15!

“When I was coming up, I didn’t know how to take proper care of my hair when I first started working in television and film,” Lopez explained on an Instagram post for the brand Hers. “I had to learn about minoxidil from my hairstylist! And I’ve been using it ever since.” (Her favorite sunscreen is a bargain, too!)

This hair treatment by Hers will give your hair a natural lift. The deep conditioning formula was made with two percent minoxidil, which is a topical solution normally used to help replenish hair loss. (And, it’s J. Lo-approved!)

Minoxidil stimulates your scalp, encouraging the shedding of weak strands and actively promoting the growth of stronger, thicker tresses. Over time, this results in a healthier, more a lustrous head of hair.

Whether you’re actively losing hair or your locks feel dry and brittle, minoxidil can help! Just apply 1 ml of the product onto your scalp twice a day and make sure it stays on for at least four hours before you wash your hair.

One reviewer raved at the “spa-like results,” saying she “saw new hair growth within a week of use. My hair seems like it has filled in and gotten thicker and richer since I have been using the Hers products. Honestly, I didn’t expect much in the beginning, but I am now convinced!”
