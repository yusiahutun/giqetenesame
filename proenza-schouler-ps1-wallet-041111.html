Proenza Schouler Introduces the PS1 in Convenient Wallet Form

If your wallet's anything like ours — a riot of crumpled Chipotle receipts and long-lost coat check tickets — you know that a beautiful exterior can camouflage a lot of chaos. Proenza Schouler's new PS1 Continental wallet, a variation on their beloved bag style, is just the thing for achieving that my-life's-under-control veneer. It comes in 5 hues, including the bold Kelly green above, and is available starting today on ProenzaSchouler.com.

Véronique Hyland is ELLE’s Fashion Features Director and the author of the book Dress Code, which was selected as one of The New Yorker's Best Books of the Year. Her writing has previously appeared in The New York Times Magazine, The New Yorker, W, New York magazine, Harper’s Bazaar, and Condé Nast Traveler.
