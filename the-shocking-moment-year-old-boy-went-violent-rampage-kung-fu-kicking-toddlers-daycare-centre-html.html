This disturbing video shows a nine-year-old boy viciously attacking other children at a daycare centre.

His violent behaviour was captured by a surveillance camera at the Kiddie City Childcare Centre in Vicksburg, Mississippi.

As a child care worker looks the other way the thug can be seen choking a young boy, repeatedly punching him in the head and biting him.

Another victim can be seen being sent flying with a kung fu style kick after the unchecked violence.

Police released the video footage after a care worker was arrested and charged with two counts of contributing to the neglect of a minor.

Sandra Trevillion,49, was arrested at her home by detectives after they viewed the tape.

An aunt of the boy, who is not named, admitted to local TV that her nephew  has anger problems while is mother said she was shocked by the level of violence.

Investigators came across the shocking video while looking into an alleged assault at the day care centre by the father of the young girl being abused.

Jamie Williams had stormed into the centre looking for the boy who allegedly caused her injuries, including a split lip and bruising on her face.

Brutal: The little girl is totally unaware as the older boy lifts his leg behind her to kick her in the back

Kung fu: He jumps up and viciously kicks the small girl mid-air, landing on the floor on his stomach

He spotted a boy he thought was responsible, but turned out not to be, and slapped him across the head. 

Williams was arrested and charged with misdemeanor simple assault.

He told WBLT TV that his year old daughter came home for about a week with injuries including a split lip and a bit mark on her chin.

He said he was angry when staff couldn't explain her injuries.

'Don't nobody know nothing at the daycare. Nobody know nothing,' said Williams. 

Police said he was wrong to carry out his own punishment but said the boy was allowed to go on the rampage hurting other children.

Violent: The boy's second victim is sitting on a bench when he goes over and starts biting and punching him

Shocking: He leans in as he attacks the young boy

'This 9-year-old child,he was allowed to pretty much run rough shot over that entire room for a period of 15 to 20 minutes. All his actions went unnoticed,' said Vicksburg Police Chief Walter Armstrong.

He said police have interviewed the mother of the boy and further charges could follow.

Officials with child services are also investigating the day care centre.

Trevillion is being held on $2,656 bond.

The boy is no longer enrolled at the centre, according to WBLT TV.
