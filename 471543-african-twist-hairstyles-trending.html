20 best African twist hairstyles that are trending in 2024

1. Versatile crochet twist braids

Twist crochet braids are well-known for their ability to be customized by weaving in extensions. In addition, crotchet twist braids are versatile because they complement any face shape and hair colour.

Crochet twist braid can last anywhere from four to eight weeks, based on the complexity of your braid trend, the quality of hair installed, and your upkeep regimen. Even though crochet braids are a long-term classic look, remember that your natural hair requires attention too.

2. Long Senegalese twists

Senegalese twists and long hair are a unique, winning combination. You can choose these super long twist hairstyles for the ultimate reggae vibes. But, of course, you always wear clip-in extensions if you don't have the length.

Senegalese twists are a protective style that keeps your hair from getting damaged. Protective styles enhance healthy hair growth by pushing you to stop things like blow drying and extreme brushing.

Are you tired of the long braid style? Shoulder-length style is just as beautiful and frequently more workable. For shoulder-length twist braids, the chunkier the twists, the greater the volume. 5. Box braids hair twist

This hairstyle looks great with black kinks and a round face shape. Box braids and twist hairstyles are ideal for all women with textured hair, especially those transitioning from relaxed to natural hair and looking for a low-maintenance look. The look can last for up to four weeks.
