See Hailey Bieber Go Casual in a Crop Top and Oversized Leather Jacket

Another Hailey Bieber street-style look, another effortlessly cool leather jacket.

The supermodel has stepped out in every iteration of the outerwear staple, from a full-length trench, to a chic fringed number, to a shiny Matrix-esque coat. On Thursday, the star was photographed during an afternoon out in Los Angeles, where she layered her favorite cold-wardrobe staple over a casual, ab-baring look.

The Rhode founder paired an oversized zip-up bomber with a black cropped tee, medium-wash straight-leg jeans, a classic black belt, and pointed-toe black boots. She accessorized the outfit with minimal jewelry, adding a pair of large gold hoops. She also wore a red Yankees cap, matching the pop of color to her glossy red manicure.

thecelebrityfinder/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Earlier this week, the star styled the casual bomber with some preppy staples, as she and husband Justin Bieber stepped out for a low-key lunch date the day after the 2023 Oscars. The couple coordinated in baggy jeans, with Hailey pairing her light blue pants with a blue Loewe polo shirt with white and red stripes, black boots with chunky heels, and a black quilted leather Miu Miu handbag. She accessorized with sleek black sunglasses, and kept her hair down and her makeup minimal.

Meanwhile, the "Peaches" singer wore vintage-inspired jeans his Drew House brand, which had the brand’s name stamped in giant yellow letters over a black patch on the sides. He paired the extra-baggy bottoms with a Drew House blueish-gray bouclé trucker jacket, a gray sweatshirt, white sneakers, and a hot pink beanie.
