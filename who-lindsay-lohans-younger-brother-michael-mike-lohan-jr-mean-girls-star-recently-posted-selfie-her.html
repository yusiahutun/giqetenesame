So what has he been up to lately?

Here’s what we know about Michael Lohan Jr:

What does Michael Lohan Jr do for a living?

According to his LinkedIn profile, the New York-born entrepreneur currently works as a managing partner for a real estate advisory firm called Nineteen Blocks Group.

He’s starred in films with his sister

Long before joining the property industry, Lohan Jr dabbled in acting just like his famous sister. Per his IMDB page, he had a cameo in The Parent Trap with his sister back in 1998, and has also played himself in E! True Hollywood Story, Living Lohan and Lindsay Lohan’s Beach Club. People has reported that, like his older sis, he was also a model for Ford as a child. But the showbiz life wasn’t for him. “I don’t like being at the forefront,” he told The New York Post. “It’s not really my cup of tea.”

He’s married

Lohan Jr tied the knot with his college sweetheart Nina Ginsberg in Rhode Island 2017, per Hello! However, his big sis couldn’t make it as she had been busy working on a “secret project”, according to the magazine at the time.

He launched a business with his famous sister

In the early 2010s, Lindsay and Mike joined forces to launch Vigme, an online shopping app that allows users to view the virtual wardrobes of celebrities and buy the items, per The Hollywood Reporter. Work on the app stalled in 2014 when a US$60 million lawsuit was brought forward claiming the idea was stolen from one of Lindsay’s ex business associates, People reported. However, the star siblings won the legal battle the following year, according to the same source.
