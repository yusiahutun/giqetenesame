Katie Holmes left Scientology but lives by simple rule when it comes to her daughter Suri

Katie Holmes and Tom Cruise’s daughter Suri is all grown up, which is hard to believe after seeing her as a child. The Batman star has revealed she likes to keep her grounded, despite having parents who are big names in showbiz.

The Dawson’s Creek alum was previously married to Tom Cruise, but the duo split in 2012 after six years of marriage. Katie Holmes shares daughter Suri with the Mission Impossible star as they co-parent the youngster, who has now turned 18. However, the celebrity revealed she didn’t want to spoil her child, and imposed a rule on herself when it came to parenting, despite her net worth and star status.

Katie Holmes’ daughter Suri turns 18

The movie star, who has been seen strolling the streets of New York City with her daughter, previously opened up about parenting and the bond they share.

When Katie’s daughter Suri was nine, Holmes explained she was already “generous and sensitive”, as well as the parenting rule she implemented throughout her childhood.

She told the Today Show: “My little one is very, very generous and very sensitive.

“So, she’s always [saying], ‘Mom, let’s give my old toys to people who need it’. So we are always doing that. I don’t over-gift.

“I don’t inundate my child with a lot of things.”

Despite the TV alum’s bank balance, she hasn’t spoiled her daughter over the years.

She also spilled to InStyle: “I love her so much. My biggest goal has been to nurture her into her individuality.

“To makes sure she is 100 per cent herself and strong, confident, and able. And to know it”.

Cruise and Holmes’ daughter Suri is set to head off to college this fall, and it’s believe she’ll be able to see mom Katie regularly if she chooses colleges in the city over traveling further afield.

Hollywood duo’s relationship timeline

Tom Cruise and Katie Holmes met in 2005, but growing up, she already had a crush on the film star.

“I’m going to marry him one day,” she says she told her sisters back in a Seventeen interview in October 2004. She was engaged to Chris Klein at the time, but it didn’t end up working out either.

Cruise spoke to Katie’s manager, saying there was a potential role for her to star in Mission Impossible: III, with the duo hitting it off.

They became another hot relationship in Hollywood when they were spotted in public together the following year. The celebrity couple tied the knot in 2006, and welcomed Suri in the April.

However, it wasn’t written in the stars as they broke it off six years later, with reports claiming Scientology was part of the decision. Cruise is a dedicated Scientologist, and Katie joined the religion when they were together. Insiders claimed the starlet didn’t want to raise their daughter in the religion, so she left and divorced the Top Gun star.

According to divorce papers, Katie Holmes was given ‘sole custody’ of Suri, and Tom paid her $400,000 in child support until she turned 18 years old.
