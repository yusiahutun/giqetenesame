Spotify Wrapped 2023 Has Arrived! Here's How to See Your Stats

Key Takeaways

  • Spotify Wrapped 2023 is finally here, revealing insights into your listening habits and favorite artists from the past year.
  • You can access your Wrapped on the Spotify app or website, and enjoy curated playlists and personalized recommendations based on your music taste.
  • New features include Sound Town, Me in 2023, personalized artist messages, and detailed information on your top artists and genres. Plus, you can share your Wrapped and create a Blend playlist with friends!

Every year, we count down to one big event. We're not talking about the holidays; that's right, we're talking about Spotify Wrapped release. Its arrival is the moment you can see your personal music trends over the last year in a fun and fresh way. Here's how to get your Spotify Wrapped 2023 report and dig in.

Spotify Wrapped 2023 is Now Live

The wait is finally over, with Spotify Wrapped 2023 rolling out, the 29th of November, 2023. If you're unfamiliar with how Spotify Wrapped works, it compiles statistics about your listening habits throughout the year, including insights on your top artists, most listened-to songs, and much more; it's an in-depth look into your year in music, brought to life by Spotify. If you use Spotify at all, it's neat to look at, but it's definitely a gift from Spotify that rewards super fans the most. The more you listen, the more information Spotify has to compile into your Wrapped report.

The Wrapped section on Spotify features curated playlists highlighting the year's top tracks and artists, as well as personalized playlists tailored to your musical taste, which you can share with your friends. In a first, Wrapped 2023 also brings your year's musical highlights to Spotify's AI DJ, who will pick and choose tracks from your most listened throughout 2023. If you haven't played with the AI DJ yet, now's the perfect time.

How to Find Your Spotify Wrapped 2023

To see your musical year summed up, you can either use Spotify's mobile app or its website. If you're using Spotify's iOS or Android app, make sure you've updated it to the latest version.

You should see a banner labeled Your 2023 Wrapped at the top of the home screen as soon as you launch Spotify. If you don't, tap the Wrapped button on the app's homepage, and you'll be redirected to another page where the banner will await you. Tap the banner and let the magic begin!

You can also view your Spotify Wrapped 2023 on your computer by heading to Spotify, logging in with your account credentials, and clicking the Check it out button at the top of the screen or by jumping right to the Spotify Wrapped page.

What's New for Spotify 2023 Wrapped?

Spotify always makes sure the current year's Wrapped is better than the previous, and 2023 is no exception, including new data on:

  • Sound Town: With this feature, Spotify correlates your listening patterns and favorite artists with a city whose residents are most likely to have similar music preferences.
  • Me in 2023: Remember "Your Listening Personality" from 2022's Spotify Wrapped, where the streaming platform took inspiration from Myers-Briggs personality types? In 2023, Spotify groups you into one of twelve different listening characters and assigns you one based on your streaming habits throughout the year. Do you believe that old is gold and prefer to listen to older tracks? You're the Time Traveler! Do you switch from one artist to the other faster than the speed of light? You're the Shapeshifter.
  • Your Artist Messages: Many artists partnered with Spotify to create personalized videos for their top listeners, expressing heartfelt gratitude or informing top listeners about upcoming tours and new releases. While this isn't a completely new feature, this year the number of artists issuing personal messages for the Wrapped event is much larger.
  • Top 5 Artists: Did you listen to Olivia Rodrigo's playlist on repeat when it was released? Well, Spotify isn't letting it go this time! In addition to showcasing your top listened-to artists, the platform will now reveal the specific month when you listened to them the most.
  • Top 5 Genres: This feature will display your top five music genres in a cute sandwich-inspired design.

Spotify Wrapped will also show you exactly how many minutes you spent streaming, how many hours you spent with your top artist, which day you peaked, and your top songs of the year in a playlist.

Once you've viewed your Spotify Wrapped for the year, you can share each slide to your social media accounts by tapping the Share button located under each slide.

If you have a group of friends who share your taste in music, you can also create a Blend playlist by heading to the Wrapped section on Spotify, tapping Create a Blend, and inviting your friends.
