David Beckham 'fangirls' at private Spice Girls reunion 12 years after last group performance

Spice Up Your Life! Victoria Beckham hosted the most epic birthday bash for her big 5-0, and we wouldn’t expect any less from Posh Spice. Even though she was on crutches, she didn’t let that stop her from having the time of her life, and there was even a Spice Girl reunion going on. We can’t cope!

A-list celebrities including Tom Cruise, Eva Longoria, and Salma Hayek attended the exclusive bash, which was hosted in London Mayfair private members’ club Oswald’s, with 100 guests partying into the early hours. They were all snapped leaving after what seemed like a great night, as David Beckham adorably gave Posh a piggyback in the early hours.

OMG, all five of the Spice Girls are back together again, and thanks to social media King David Beckham, we were given a glimpse of the music performance.

In the video, shared to the former soccer player’s Instagram, the Fab Five can be seen singing and dancing to their hit track, Stop. Like us, David couldn’t hold his excitement as he was hilariously seen ‘fangirling’ and singing along to the camera.

“I mean come on x,” he wrote in the caption, tagging all five members, Victoria, Mel C, Mel B, Geri Halliwell, and Emma Bunton.

The girls embarked on a European Tour in 2019, however, Victoria did not join them, so it’s the first time fans have seen all five perform together since the 2012 London Olympics. And looking at the former Manchester United player’s Instagram comments, they were loving every second.

“David you are the best social media manager out there, thank you for giving the people CONTENT,” wrote one excited fan girl.

“The moment the entire planet has been waiting for,” wrote another.

A third laughed: “I am crying ’cause David’s the groupie here.”

“The way a whole generation is SHAKING,” exclaimed a fourth.

Posh arrives at her event on crutches

In February, the former Spice Girl revealed she had taken a tumble in the gym, which had led to a break in her foot.

It seems like the injury hasn’t yet fully healed as the star was seen entering her party on crutches, but she was still rocking it.

The newly turned 50-year-old stunned in a sheer mint dress, which hugged her waistline. She wore her long brunette hair in effortless waves and accessorized with gold and silver bracelets.
