Vanessa Hudgens and Paris Jackson at Thriller Night Halloween party

Vanessa Hudgens and Paris Jackson led stars down the red carpet at Prince Jackson's Thriller Night Halloween party in Encino, California on Friday night.

Channeling the Black Swan, Hudgens wowed in a plunging gown covered in black feathers that highlighted her ample chest.

Several feathery strands cascaded down onto the train of the dress that pooled around the High School Musical alum as she posed for shutterbugs.

Star-studded: Vanessa Hudgens and Paris Jackson led stars down the red carpet at Prince Jackson's Thriller Night Halloween party in Encino, California on Friday night 

Her toned legs and platform black patent leather boots peeked out through a daring thigh-high slit.

For some added drama, Hudgens rocked bold black eyeshadow blended in the shape of bird wings. Her lips were painted a glossy black and her cheeks were dusted with shimmery bronzer for a radiant appearance.
