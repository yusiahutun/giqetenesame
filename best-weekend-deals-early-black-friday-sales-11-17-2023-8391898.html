Lululemon

Lululemon’s under-the-radar We Made Too Much section is where you’ll find the best steals on some of the athletic brand’s most popular pieces. But given you’ll rarely find Lululemon at these lower prices, sizes tend to go out of stock quickly. That’s why we sorted through the section to find well-stocked staples your workout wardrobe needs, including the popular Align leggings starting at just $39, a “comfortable, moveable, functional, and fashionable” $29 tank top, and a 33 percent-off sports bra. And for winter workouts, I’m personally eyeing this fleece pullover customers have said is “soft,” “cozy,” “chic,” and “so warm.”

Tula

Tula has quickly become a go-to beauty brand for InStyle editors; our team is obsessed with everything from its under-eye balms to its just-launched concealer. And right now, everything is 30 percent off for reward members — to sign up and take advantage of the sale, all you need to provide is an email and a password. If you’re new to Tula, I’d recommend using this sale to stock up on the brand’s best-sellers, including the tinted SPF a shopper over 65 said provides “excellent coverage” without settling into their fine lines.

You can also grab this now-$41 nourishing moisturizer that has more than 3,900 five-star reviews, as well as its lightly tinted primer shoppers say “smooths, hydrates, and blurs” their skin. And if you’re looking for an anti-aging holy grail, customers over 45 say that the brand’s wrinkle serum “really erased [their] fine lines,” adding that they now have a “much younger looking glow.”

Tory Burch

Tory Burch officially launched its Holiday Event sale today, with up to 67 percent off select styles. As part of the event, you can take advantage of major markdowns plus an extra 30 percent off $250 or more at checkout. As someone who loves high-quality designer pieces — but doesn’t love the typical price tag — I know I’ll be taking advantage of this early deal. And the first item I’m grabbing are the sexy (but practical) Georgia Bombé Slingback Pumps, which customers have called the “most versatile shoes” they own. And if you really want to step your shoe game up, you can snag these trending knee high black boots for a whopping $439 off.

In terms of bags, you can’t go wrong with the classic Kira Tote, which shoppers describe as “simple,” “classy and elegant looking.” This style is eligible for an extra 30 percent off at checkout, which saves you a total of 51 percent off the original price. You can also snag the iconic Ella Printed Tote in a number of designs starting at $179. This ivory and black option with neutral stripes is bound to go with everything in your wardrobe; shoppers say it’s “very chic” and the “best tote bag ever.” Plus, the large Tory Burch logo doubles as a snap pocket on the front of the bag.

I recently grabbed one of the brand’s ultra-soft, perfectly-fitted hoodies and have only taken it off to wash it since, so I’ll absolutely be grabbing another while it’s on sale for just $30. Other essentials I’m eyeing? This up to 82-percent-off (!) cotton turtleneck that shoppers say is super comfy, “high-end” looking, and breathable, this $29 pack of scoop-neck long-sleeve tees, and these wide-leg jeans that “flatter” customers’ curves “like no other,” which you can get for $18 at checkout.
