Are the tattoos real on Bosch's arms? Although Welliver has several tattoos, the ones most evident on the show are his sleeve tattoos. So did the show decide to pull a Michael Scofield and draw them on, or are they real sleeves? In an interview with the Tampa Bay Times in 2017, the actor revealed that every tattoo he has is real. "The tattoos are real. They're mine."

You may wonder how the actor wore his real art on the show. Well, Welliver has an interesting story about how this came to pass. In an interview with Inked in 2018, he revealed that the decision was made after he was cast as Bosch. He mentions that he told Michael Connely that covering the tattoos would take extra time daily. After which, they decided to keep them.

Titus Welliver tattoos and their meanings

What tattoos does Titus Welliver have? And what do they mean? Unfortunately, although it is common knowledge that he has over 30 tattoos, not all of them are publicly visible. Therefore, it is impossible to tell the meanings of all his tattoos. However, given that he has not provided a catalogue, here are some of Welliver's tattoos and what they mean.

1. Two koi fish circling a rock

The Bosch actor has an image of two koi fish circling a rock on his shoulder. In his interview with Inked, he revealed that this image was his first tattoo. It is the symbol of his martial arts dojo. The two koi circling a rock represent fluidity.

2. The Silver Surfer

If you didn't know before that the actor was a comic book fan, now you do. Right below his first tattoo, Titus has a tattoo of the Silver Surfer from Marvel Comics. The Silver Surfer is a humanoid alien with metallic skin who can travel through space with the aid of his surfboard-like craft.

Apart from the character's back story and his level of fame as a comic book character, the Silver Surfer image makes for an excellent tattoo. It is framed by a flower tattoo to the left and a few words to the right.

3. White pony

On his right arm still, the talented actor has a small depiction of a pony galloping into the distance. Right on top of the horse, the words 'So it goes' are written, followed by an ellipsis. Looking at the actor, you may wonder what such an image might mean to him.

Surprisingly, the actor reveals that the tattoo is a tribute to his favourite band. He reveals on Spin that his ink was inspired by the cover of the Deftones' White Pony album.

4. The wildcats

Numerous tattoo designs cover Welliver's sleeves. On his right forearm, he has a tattoo of three wildcats, top to bottom. The first one from the top is a lion's head, the second one is a tiger's face, and the third one is the face of a bobcat. The actor later covered the lion's head with a tattoo of a rose flower.

Although he hasn't revealed what they mean, many people use lion and tiger tattoos to convey the wearer's bravery and courage.

5. Weapons

To the left of the three wildcat heads, he has a tattoo of a closed fist, followed by a stick, a revolver, and a knife. Welliver has not yet publicly revealed the meaning of these tattoos. But, the two most common meanings people associate with their gun tattoos are strength and power.

6. Bear and Wolf

On the inside of his forearm is another tattoo of the face of a bear and the letters NTBFW. Right next to them is a wolf tattoo on the inside of his right wrist with the words Lone Wolf written above them.

The three tattoos have different meanings, although they are slightly related. The bear tattoo represents courage and endurance. The letters, initials for the slang "Not To Be F*cked With", have a self-explanatory meaning. The wolf represents the loyalty and love he has for his pack.

7. Star tattoo

Right below the bobcat's head on his right wrist, the actor has an image of a blue and black star. Star wrist tattoos usually represent bravery and having a good direction in life.

8. All ways, Always

On his upper right forearm, Welliver has the words All Ways and Always tattooed in a logo-like fashion. This phrase is used to define being present through something unconditionally.

9. Icons

On the back of his left forearm, the actor has tattooed several icons together consisting of eyes, arrows, and hearts. The icons are drawn together in a straight line suggesting a connection. The symbols allegedly represent the words “I’ll eat you up I love you so.”

10. Stag

One of the predominant tattoos on the actor's left forearm is this stag tattoo. Stag symbolism and meanings include stamina, virility, grace, instincts, maturity, regeneration, and spiritual enlightenment. The stag image is most commonly used as it is exalted among other deer.
