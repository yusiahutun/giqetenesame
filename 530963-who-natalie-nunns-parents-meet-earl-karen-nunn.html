Who is Natalie Nunn?

She is a renowned American reality TV personality. Natalie was born on 26 December 1984 in Concord, California, United States, to Earl and Karen Nunn. She has one sibling, Ronald Nunn. Unlike her celebrity sister, Ronald prefers leading a more private life.

Natalie holds an American nationality with a mixed-racial background. Her ethnicity is a mixture of Puerto Rican, Brazilian, and African-American. Even though she was born in Concord, she grew up in Pleasanton, Alameda County.

After primary school, she joined Aragon High School in San Mateo, where she ran in the school's track team and competed in the Junior Olympics in 2002. Later, she enrolled at the University of Southern California and was a Trojans women's soccer team defender.

The TV personality's parents are Karen Nunn and Earl Nunn. She has a close relationship with her father and mother. She frequently shares photos and messages of appreciation and love for them on her social media accounts.
