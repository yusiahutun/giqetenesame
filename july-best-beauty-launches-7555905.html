The 12 Best July 2023 Beauty Launches

In the midst of summer, you might assume things are slow by way of new beauty products. As it turns out, it's the complete opposite. From new innovations in skin care to inspiring new trends like latte makeup, July has proven to be a very exciting month for newness.

We've got Hourglass' first foray into skin tints, a viral sunscreen that's finally made its way to the States, and a liquid pimple patch that is going to change the way you approach acne treatment for the better. Plus, there are plenty of fun things for all of your makeup and hair needs.

Below, get acquainted with the 12 best beauty products that launched this month.
