Natalie Morales' biography

The actress was born on 15th February 1985 in Kendall, Florida, USA. She is the daughter of Mario Morales Junior (father) and Penelope Morales (mother). Who are Natalie Morales' siblings? The American actress has not offered any information about her siblings.

She began her studies at the St Agatha Catholic School for her elementary education. Natalie then enrolled at Southwest Miami Senior High School for her high school studies. Later, she joined the University of Miami's Dow Jones Minority High School Journalism Workshop.

Career

The actress began her career by making a guest appearance on the CSI: Miami TV show in 2006. She played the same role in a video game version of the MTV television series titled Pimp My Ride during the same year.

Her first significant role was in the science fiction comedy-drama on ABC Family network, The Middleman season one starring as Wendy Watson. In 2008, she was part of the executive producers of the Quitter web series. It was an official selection of the 3rd annual Independent Television Festival in August 2008 in Los Angeles, California.
