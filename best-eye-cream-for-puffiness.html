Which ingredients best help treat puffy eyes?

Caffeine, hyaluronic acid, peptides, arnica, and chamomile are commonly used to treat puffy eyes. These ingredients are key in helping to minimize inflammation and fluid retention.

What are some additional ways to treat under-eye puffiness?

Aside from reaching for one of the best eye creams for puffiness, there are a few other methods you can try. Henry says that you can use a cool compress, elevate your head while sleeping to prevent fluid retention, get adequate hydration, reduce sodium intake, address allergies, and get quality sleep.
