What do local news anchors do?

News anchors are broadcast journalists who report news live from television studios or radio stations. They develop stories by researching current events and interviewing various subjects. They also write stories and scripts to be aired by their respective stations.

There are various skills that a news anchor requires. They include;

  • Proven work experience as a news anchor
  • Broad knowledge of local, national and international affairs (e.g. political, economic, legislative and cultural)
  • Understanding of the newscast process
  • Comfortable yet professional presence on camera
