County Democrats to meet March 23 | Free News

Jones County Democrats are scheduled to host a county convention on Saturday, March 23, at 10 a.m. at the Jones County courthouse in Laurel.

All interested Democrats are encouraged to attend.

The event will include election of party leaders and discussions on issues for the party platform.

Jones Jackson at 601-433-1027 or email fjackson@bellsouth.net.
