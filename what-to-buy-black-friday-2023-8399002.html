People Always Ask Me What to Buy During Black Friday 2023

Black Friday is always a lot of pressure for me — not only is it the busiest time of year as a shopping editor, but everyone ends up texting me about deals. “What should I get from Amazon?” "How about Nordstrom?" “Is this deal any good?” “What are you actually buying?” Those are just some of the questions I get. I usually try to be as honest as possible, whether that’s confirming that yes, that deal is the best you’ll get all year or no, that product isn’t worth buying even on sale.

But the easiest solution? Putting together a list of the things that have my stamp of approval for the entire internet to see, spanning across fashion and beauty. Below are the eight Black Friday deals I definitely think are worth shopping this year, from a salon-quality shampoo that’ll actually improve your strands to the best sweatshirt you’ll ever buy:

I’ve waxed poetic about Ewedoos’ Fleece-Lined Leggings before, and I’ll do it again (and again). I truly live in the cozy leggings once the temperature drops below 50 degrees. Not only do they keep me warm on long winter walks, but they’re super comfy and go with everything, too. I like to pair them with a long puffer jacket, high ankle socks, and my New Balance kicks. But don’t just take it from me — more than 10,500 Amazon shoppers have left them a five-star rating. At just $20 right now, they’re a total steal.
