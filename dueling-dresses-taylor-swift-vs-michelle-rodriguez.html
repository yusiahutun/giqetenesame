Taylor Swift vs. Michelle Rodriguez!

Which star rocked the hot Herve Leger by Max Azria bandage dress better?

Taylor Swift wore the hip-hugging black and white dress to the 2013 MuchMusic Video Awards on Sunday.

She paired the mini with Christian Louboutin booties – to die for! The singer sported curly blonde locks and completed her look with red lipstick.

Michelle Rodriguez donned a similar (but longer) version of the dress back in May at the "Fast and Furious 6" premiere in Seoul.

The actress took a more natural approach, wearing light makeup and rocking wavy hair. Michelle finished her outfit with a simple silver cuff.

We have to give the nod to Taylor for looking sexy and totally ready for summer. Plus, Rodriguez's hair was a little too casual for us.

But it's your vote that counts!
