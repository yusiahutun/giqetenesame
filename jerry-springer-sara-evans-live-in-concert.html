Jerry Springer &amp; Sara Evans Live &amp; In Concert

Merrillville (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Former Cincinnati Mayor and lurid talk-show host Jerry Spring will appear in a concert with country singer Sara Evans, Nashville news outlet WKRN reported.

Both Evans and Spring had recently appeared on ABC's hit program "Dancing with the stars" where they had been cast as rivals. Disgraced former Texas congressman Tom Delay had urged republican voters to cast a ballot for Evans as a vote for Springer would be tantamount to a vote for smut.

Evans dropped out of the show due to an apparently acrimonious divorce but she and Springer are now planning to appear together at a Merrillville, Indiana on December 1.

Springer has recently put an album together and even appeared at the Grand Old Opry. – CelebrityAccess Staff Writers
