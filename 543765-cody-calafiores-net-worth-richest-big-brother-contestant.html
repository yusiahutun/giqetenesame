What does Cody Calafiore do now?

Following his appearance in Big Brother 16 (2014), Cody signed with Soul Artist Management, a New York-based modelling agency. He has since appeared in several magazines, including Risbel, Men's Fitness, Love, Wing and more. Cody has appeared in fashion shows as a model for Ricardo Seco, Gents and Malan Breton.

In July 2021, he launched a podcast called The Winner’s Circle with fellow Big Brother winner Derrick Levasseur. They discuss the current Big Brother season.

In January 2023, he appeared as a contestant in the first season of Peacocks reality TV show The Traitors.

Cody Calafiore education

Cody Calafiore attended Monmouth University, where he played soccer. He then transferred to Temple University, graduating with a Bachelor in Business Administration. He played soccer for Temple Owls from 2010 to 2012. He went for trials at Columbus Crew during preseason, but an ankle injury ended his soccer career.

Is Cody Calafiore married?

On October 22, 2022, the Big Brother alum Cody announced his engagement to his girlfriend of seven years, Cristie Laratta. On February 16, 2024, they tied the knot in New Jersey, where they were both raised – and first met. They celebrated their nuptials with 200 loved ones at Shadowbrook, Shrewsbury.
