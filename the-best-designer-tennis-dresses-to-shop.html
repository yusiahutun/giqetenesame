The 7 best designer tennis dresses to shop now

Make a statement both on and off the court...

    Ever since Zendaya gave us a lesson in preppy tennis spectator style last week, we haven't been able to stop thinking about how tenniscore would be a perfect spring-style adoption. 

    Obviously, tennis-based ensembles have been around for decades, made famous by the likes of Wimbledon, Its iconic all-white dress code has influenced elegant and minimalist styles, while its sporty and active atmosphere has sparked the popularity of tennis-inspired fashion trends, such as pleated skirts and polo shirts.

    In recent months tennis-inspired garments have made a stylistic comeback leading us to believe that the aesthetic is going to be everywhere this spring and summer. An array of sporty style adaptations were seen during fashion month A/W24, some donned on the street by famous faces and others on the catwalk of notable brands such as Miu Miu and Lacoste.  "As racket sports rise in popularity on and off the court (and on the big screen), we’re seeing people head to Pinterest to search for racket-ready fits before the season gets into full swing," explains Jenna Waller, Head of Fashion at Pinterest UK, "From tennis skirts and sweater vests to trainers and polo tops, the classic looks offer a preppy uniform for all occasions, with many 'saving' a selection of these to add to their upcoming outfits."

    Like all good trends, we like to jump on them early, mainly to ensure we're classed under the cool category but also because we're scared of getting FOMO when everything sells out. If you're like us, let this be your sign to invest in a designer tennis dress worthy of a Wimbledon win. From elegant A-line silhouettes to sleek, form-fitting styles, these dresses are tailored to flatter your figure and enhance your confidence both on and off the court.

    Whether you prefer a classic and timeless look or a modern and edgy vibe, there's a designer tennis dress that matches your personal style. Elevate your game and stand out with these exceptional creations that blend fashion-forward design with athletic functionality.

    How do I style a tennis dress for maximum chic?

    Tennis dresses are already ultra-chic by design, but keep the preppy theme going strong this season with chunky white trainers, and artfully scrunched socks. For a court to casual, keep things fresh with a denim jacket or chic bomber.
