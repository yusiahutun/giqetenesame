Who is Marlo Hampton?

Marlo Hampton was born on 7 February 1976 in St. Petersburg, Florida, USA. She is a talented Hollywood actress famous for her appearances in Caught in the Act: Unfaithful (2022), Boss Moves with Rasheeda (2022), and Games People Play (2019).

Marlo joined the Real Housewives of Atlanta in season four as a recurring cast member, but she was made a main character in season 14.

She was raised in the foster care system as a child because of her mom's drug abuse and addiction. Despite her hard life, Marlo graduated in 2003 with a BA in social work and interdisciplinary science from the University of Southern Florida. Years later, she founded a high-end fashion boutique for the Atlanta elite.

Marlo also started the Glam It Up project to motivate young girls raised in the foster care system to excel and reach their top potential.
