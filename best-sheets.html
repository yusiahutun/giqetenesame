Best Standard Sheets

Brooklinen Luxe Core Sheet Set

Best Standard Sheets

Brooklinen Luxe Core Sheet Set

Credit: Brooklinen

"You know the story of the princess and the pea? I am that princess: I can’t sleep unless the conditions are perfect. I’ve been known to get out of a hotel bed in the middle of the night and remake it with hospital corners—it’s that bad. Brooklinen’s luxe sheets meet my very high standards. They feel great on your skin—soft and satiny—and have not pilled in over a year of use. I only wish they came in more fun prints (although the cream is very classy.)" —Izzy Grinspan, deputy digital editor

Best Textured Sheets

Tekla Percale Bedding

Best Textured Sheets

Tekla Percale Bedding

"I am obsessed with Tekla. I own sheets, bathrobes, pajamas, and towels by the brand and I still can't get enough. While I obsessively shop over clothing and accessories, I've never felt an immense brand loyalty for anything in the home space—until I discovered Tekla. The texture of everything is so luxurious, it reminds me of the kind of sheets you wake up in on vacation. The colors they use are also very soothing, like the pastels of a sunset in your favorite far flung location." —Tara Gonzalez, senior fashion editor

Best Eccentric Sheets

Gata Big waves

Best Eccentric Sheets
