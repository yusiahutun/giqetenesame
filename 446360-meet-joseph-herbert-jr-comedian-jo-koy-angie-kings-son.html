Early life

Joseph Herbert Jr. (Lil Joe) was born on April 21, 2003, making him 18 years old as of 2022. He is the son of famous parents with his Filipino-American father, Joseph Glenn Herbert Sr. being, a comedian, and his mother, Angie King, a singer.

His parents started dating back in the early 2000s and exchanged their vows to become husband and wife in 2005. However, their marriage did not last as they divorced in 2013 and agreed on joint custody of their son. Despite the divorce, they remained good friends and even quarantined together during the Covid19 pandemic.

Lil Joe's mother is dating artist Gino Perez while his father is dating an actress named Chelsea Handler. Lil grew up in a devout Catholic family and was enrolled in a catholic junior school. After graduating, he joined St. Mel School in Woodlands, California.
