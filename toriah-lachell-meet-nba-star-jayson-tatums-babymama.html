Toriah Lachell: Meet NBA Star Jayson Tatums Babymama

Toriah Lachell is a famous entrepreneur, hair stylist, and social media sensation. However, she rose to prominence as the ex-girlfriend of the NBA player for the Boston Celtics, Jayson Tatum. Tatum is famous for his basketball skills, and his fast-rising popularity has earned him media attention.

Consequently, Tatum’s fame has shone a light on his ex-girlfriend and mother of his son, Toriah Lachell. Hence, this has made fans curious and has made her the subject of numerous stories. However, we have separated fiction from facts.

Here’s everything we know about Jayson Tatum’s ex-girlfriend.

Who Is Toriah Lachell?

Toriah Lachell is the ex-girlfriend of NBA star Jayson Tatum. She was born in St. Louis, Missouri, United States of America, in February 2000 to her mother, Julie Gibbs.

Although she has not revealed details about her elementary education, online reports suggest she attended McCluer North High School in Florissant, Missouri, United States. She played varsity soccer and softball on the school’s team for four years. 

While the details of her biological father remain a mystery, Lachell grew up with her stepfather, John Gibbs. Similarly, there are no details regarding her siblings, as she has not mentioned having any. 

How Did Toriah Lachell Meet Jayson Tatum?

Although the details of how the former couple met are yet to be determined, we know they met in high school. While Lachell was at McCluer North High School, Tatum attended Chaminade College Preparatory School in Creve Coeur, Missouri. They connected after meeting, fell in love, and started dating.

At the time, the young lovers were both known as talented athletes among their peers. They dated throughout high school and maintained their relationship even after high school. However, things went sour between the two when Lachell became pregnant. 

Why Did Toriah Lachell End Her Relationship With Jayson Tatum?

Although the former couple gave no reason for their break up, they parted ways officially after the birth of their son. Lachell became pregnant with Tatum’s child during his rookie year in the NBA.

Toriah Lachell and Jayson Tatum welcomed their child, Jayson Tatum Jr ., on December 6, 2017. However, the arrival of their son did not change the inevitable.  

Does Toriah Lachell Have Sole Custody of Jayson Tatum Jr.?

Yes, she does. However, she shares custody with her ex-boyfriend. Unlike most celebrity separations, the former couple parted ways amicably, and Lachell agreed to remain co-parents with the fast-rising NBA star so that their child would enjoy the presence of both parents in his life while growing up. 

Consequently, Lachell resolved to relocate to Boston so her son would grow up to enjoy the presence of his father in his life. The former couple are both committed to raising their child. Hence, the young celebrity kid often appears with his father at games and spends time with the NBA player. 

Is Jayson Tatum’s Ex-Girlfriend Dating?

Since Lachell and Tatum went their separate ways in 2017,  there has been no indication that Toriah Lachell has been dating anyone. The mother of one has been working on growing her enterprise and raising her son. 

However, Tatum, on the other hand, has been the subject of numerous dating rumors. Although unconfirmed, the NBA star was reportedly in a relationship with Duke volleyball player Samie Amos.

Since that ended, Tatum has been in the news for dating famous British singer Ella Mai. They sparked dating rumors after eagle-eyed fans noticed they spent time together in the same apartment. Although Jayson Tatum and Ella Mai have not confirmed the dating rumors, they have not denied it either! 

What Does Jayson Tatum’s Ex Do for a Living?

Toriah Lachell is a certified professional hair specialist and entrepreneur. She owns a high-end hair salon called The Curl Bar Boston and has managed to build a successful business.

Despite not being in the entertainment scene, she has many celebrity clients. Even the famous comedian, Trevor Noah, visited her salon and posed for a picture after getting his hair done by Lachell.

What Is Toriah Lachell’s Net Worth?

While some reports suggest Toriah Lachell has a net worth of $100,000, others suggest it is about $2,000,000. Consequently, her precise net worth is unknown as she keeps a low profile away from the media. 

However, her baby daddy, on the other hand, has an estimated net worth of $25,000,000 from his earnings as a professional basketball player.
