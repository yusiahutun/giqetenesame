Homa Bay: 3 Family Members Die after Electrocution from Clothesline

Amos Khaemba, a journalist at TUKO.co.ke, brings more than three years of experience covering politics and current affairs in Kenya.

Homa Bay - Residents of Nyatwere village in Homa Bay County have been thrown into mourning after three members of a family were electrocuted to death at their home.

Citizen Digital reported that two brothers identified as Elijah Otieno and Isaiah Otieno together with their sister-in-law Laventer Otieno died instantly because of electrocution.

Chief of Kachien North location Jacob Adienge said Laventer was electrocuted first while removing her clothes from a metallic wire used to dry clothes.

The clothesline was in contact with the iron sheet from her house, which appeared to have a flowing current because of poor power connection.

The chief said that the woman was struck immediately she touched the metallic clothesline and screamed for help before she died on the spot.

How electricity killed local barber in Kisumu

In another sad incident, residents of Kona Mbuta in the Manyatta area are reeling in shock following the tragic death of a local barber.

A part-time electrician, the barber, was reportedly struck by electricity to death after he climbed an electricity pole trying to fix the electricity cables that had caused a blackout.

According to TV47, the incident happened at around 9pm on Tuesday, February 13.
