Molly Stout - InStyle

Title: Digital Content Director

Education: Lafayette College

Location: New York, NY

Expertise: Celebrities, Fashion

  • Molly started as an Executive Editor for InStyle and then transitioned into the Digital Content Director to oversee all of the digital content.
  • Previously, she was the Entertainment Director for Refinery29 and the Pop Culture Director for Cosmopolitan Magazine.

Experience

Molly has over 18 years of experience as a writer and editor. She joined InStyle in 2020 as an Executive Editor to oversee all beauty coverage on the site. She was then promoted to Digital Content Director to focus on all InStyle's digital content. Molly previously worked for Refinery29 and Cosmopolitan.

Education

Molly received a Bachelor's degree in English from Lafayette College.
