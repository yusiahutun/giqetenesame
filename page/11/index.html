Meaning of Angel In Blue Jeans by Train

published on October 04, 2024
In Train's mesmerizing song "Angel In Blue Jeans", the mysterious and enigmatic nature of love takes center stage. The song tells a tale of a fleeting encounter with a woman, referred to as the "angel in blue jeans". Despite the narrator's ignorance of her true identity and only encountering her briefly, he finds himself infatuated and deeply in love with her.
