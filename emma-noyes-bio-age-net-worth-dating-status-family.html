Meet Emma Noyes! Her Age, Net Worth, Dating Status, Family

Recently, TikToker Emma Noyes posted a funny skit with her older brother, which went viral on the platform and invited some unwanted attention to her brother. Several of Emma’s fans went frenzy over him, using ‘beekeeping age’, a TikTok word for DILF or Zaddy, for him.

In the wiki article below, we will take a look at TikToker Emma Noyes’ bio and obviously about the recent incident that happened with her and her older brother.

Emma Noyes Age

As per radaris.com, Emma Noyes turned 27 years old in September 2022.

Emma Noyes Family

Emma comes from a big family. Her parents are Susan and Nicholas Noyes.

Susan, 66, is an “Entrepreneur, mother, publisher, philanthropist, connector, creator, writer, lover of life” as per her Facebook bio. Talking about her work, she is a publisher at Make It Better, North Shore. Formerly, she worked as an attorney at Sidley & Austin. She studied Economics and Political Science at Miami University and Law at Indiana University (Bloomington).

Emma’s father, Nicholas, turned 75 years old in August 2022. It seemed like Emma’s parents were still together as of this writing as Susan posted about her dear husband very recently. Susan shared a series of pictures of Nicholas making different faces with the caption “And I love him more every day! Hope he makes you laugh and brings you joy too” on Instagram in November 2022.

Emma has a lot of siblings–one sister and 4 brothers. Emma’s sister, Skatie, 32, is an entrepreneur. She is the owner and designer of a clothing brand called Skatie.

Her brothers are James, Patrick, John, and Nicholas. Patrick, 37, is the former assistant director of Diving at Northwestern University; John formerly worked as an Assistant Store Manager at Will Leather Goods and as a Front of House Staff at Greenleaf Gourmet Chopshop; and Nicholas, 39, is an investor and developer who studied real estate and philosophy at Cornell University.

How Much Is Emma Noyes Net Worth?

Much of Emma’s fans already knew that Emma had older siblings–she’d even featured one of her older brothers on her TikToks. However, it was when she revealed her other older brother in a recent video that it went viral.

The video was supposed to be a funny skit about her brother being much much older than her. In no time, the comment section of that particular TikTok video was filled with thirsty comments from Emma’s older brother. “When he’s your brother but also he’s my daddy,” read one fan’s comment.

As of this writing, the TikTok video had garnered over 25 million views. Responding to people’s comments about her older brother being of ‘beekeeping age,’ Emma posted another TikTok video.

Before the video about her brother went viral, Emma mostly posted videos showcasing her love for The Vampire Diaries and her day-to-day life with her fiance and dog.

Besides being a TikTok star, Emma is also an author. She wrote a young adult fantasy novel series called The Sunken City. Her upcoming novel, Guy’s Girl, which is about true love, self-love, and growing up, is in the works.

Before Emma pursued writing professionally, she worked at ZX Ventures (July 2018 to January 2021). As a Global Communications Manager there, she played a key role in developing external & internal communications and content creation.

Furthermore, Emma’s past jobs also included stints at Avero (as an Intern), Spoon University (as Editorial Director), and Make It Better Media (as an Intern).

The viral TikTok star has a bachelor’s degree in History and Literature from Harvard University. Emma also attended New Trier High School.

As of 2023, Emma’s net worth was estimated to be around $1 million.

Emma Noyes Dating Status

For all the boys who’d been crushing hard on Emma, there’s bad news ahead. The TikTok star is happily engaged to her Pontus Gitselov–and is not seeing anyone now.

Emma and Pontus reportedly met each other in early 2022 in Los Angeles. After only two or three months of dating, Emma and Pontus moved to the latter’s hometown in Sweden.

On February 7, 2023, the two celebrated their first year of togetherness. Also, on March 12th, the couple got engaged as Emma shared pictures from their surprise engagement on her Instagram.

Emma and Pontus are absolutely smitten with each other and their social media is living proof of that. Although the duo had no kids as of this writing, they co-parent their dog, Mchawi.

Talking about Emma’s man, Pontus works as a credit analyst at Wintrust Bank Chicago. He completed his MBA from Biola University Crowell School of Business in December 2022. The Swedish man also has a bachelor’s degree in Business/Managerial Economics from Maryville University of Saint Louis.
