Caring Fields Felines Fundraiser

Dr. Jim Strine, Pauline Glover and Andrea Strine

Caring Fields Felines held its annual “Merry Meows Festival” fundraiser featuring food, drink, music and a silent auction. More than 250 guests came out to support the no-kill feline rescue and adoption group located in Palm City, bringing in more than $50,000 for the organization.
