How do you play paranoia, the game?

There are several ways of playing the game, and you can customize the game to fit your team and the environment. First, whisper a question to the person on your right. Then, the person points or calls out the other person they think is the answer.

Whoever gets called has to drink if they want to find out what you asked about them. The recipient has to point at someone they think or know does this. You or them will then reveal your question to the entire group after being called out or pointed at drinks up.

The second version of the game is whispering the question to someone next to you, then let them pick who they think should answer it. Again, the other person may or may not drink up to know the question.

Please note that the questions should not necessarily lead to an answer that is a person. It can be a Yes/No answer, or someone can speak up. You can even have them making gestures or state digits. Therefore, give the person clear answering instructions.
