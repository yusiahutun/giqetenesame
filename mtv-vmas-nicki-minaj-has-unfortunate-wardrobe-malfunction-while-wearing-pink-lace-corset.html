MTV VMAs: Nicki Minaj has unfortunate wardrobe malfunction while wearing pink lace corset

Nicki Minaj is stunning fans in a Barbie bride look for the VMAs 2023 as she reveals how she faced a wardrobe malfunction.

The 2023 edition of MTV Video Music Awards served some memorable moments. From Olivia Rodrigo’s faux stage collapse act to Taylor Swift making history, fans had a lot to be excited for over the event! On top of that, Nicki Minaj stole the show as she came in a pink look to host the awards bash for the second time.

Nicki Minaj has unfortunate wardrobe malfunction while wearing pink lace corset

Nicki Minaj revealed her unfortunate wardrobe malfunction on the VMAs stage night as she came to host the event in a pink corset look. Minaj’s daring look for the night featured a transparent flowing skirt, silky pink pants, a long veil, and a pink corset.

While she looked stunning, channeling her inner Barbie, the 40-year-old rapper revealed what happened before appearing on the stage.

Nicki gave a shout-out to the rapper Lil Wayne and singer Olivia Rodrigo and revealed to fans that her shoe was coming off. “I just realized that one of my motherf***ing shoes wasn’t buckled properly,” the musician shared.

Nicki Minaj introduces new Pink Friday 2 Song

Nicki Minaj’s pink look was well-planned as she introduced a new song from her album Pink Friday 2 and also performed Last Time I Saw You at the 2023 MTV VMAs.

The rap queen lived up to her reputation with the new song spitting bars as she rapped: “It’s a big difference between me and you/ I ain’t nothing like you, you, you or you.”

Pink Friday 2 will be Nicki’s fifth studio album and it will be released on November 17, 2023 globally. After a glimpse of what Nicki has in store for music fans, the wait till November will be agonizing but well worth it!

Gushing on the upcoming album, a fan posted on X (formerly Twitter): “I loveeee the direction pink Friday 2 is going. I’m so happy!”
