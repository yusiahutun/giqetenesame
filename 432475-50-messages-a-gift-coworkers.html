Sample thank you notes for coworkers

Many people underestimate the power of a simple handwritten appreciation note. It says that you went out of your way to sit down and write to those who did something for you. Here are some samples of thank you notes for coworkers.

Sample note one

Dear .....,

I wanted to write to you to thank you again for putting a lot of thought into this special birthday gift. Your thoughtful present added lots of fun to my day, and I was touched beyond words.

I hope I can return the favour to you sometime in the future. I appreciate you!

Sincerely,

[Your name]

Sample note two

Dear .....,

Thank you so much for being part of my special day and for the beautiful birthday present. What a thoughtful gift!

I appreciate your thoughtfulness and for taking time out of your busy schedule to attend my party. I appreciate.

Best regards,

[Your name]

Sample note three

Dear .....,

I was overwhelmed with the present. Thank you for making our wedding memorable with your presence and thoughtful gift. I appreciate your generosity.

Regards!

[Your name]
