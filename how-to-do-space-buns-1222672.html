What Are Space Buns?

After a stint of popularity in the '90s, space buns have made a triumphant comeback and are currently dominating the hair world with their stylish presence. Space buns are a trendy and playful hairstyle where the hair is divided into two sections and twisted or braided into buns on each side of the head. The buns are typically positioned high on the head. From sleek and polished to messy and textured, this two-buns hairstyle can be worn in a variety of styles. Moreover, space buns are flexible and can be modified to suit your individual style. This fun hairdo can be made with any hair length and texture.

How to Do Space Buns?

Check out the list of essential tools and products that are required for this hairstyle:

  • Bobby pins or hair elastics to secure and hold the space buns in place.
  • Rat-tail comb to create precise partings and sections in your hair.
  • Hairbrush or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and ensure smooth styling.
  • Hair spray to hold and keep your space buns in place throughout the day.

Step-by-Step Instructions to Create Double Bun Hairstyle

  • Start with clean, dry hair. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly to ensure a fresh foundation for styling. Use a brush or comb to detangle your hair and remove any knots or tangles.
  • Divide your hair in half. You can either create a middle parting or a side parting, depending on your style and preference. Make sure whatever parting you are using, divide your hair into two equal sections.
  • Now, take one section of hair and temporarily secure it with a hair tie or clip to keep it out of the way while you work on the other section.
  • By using a comb, take the remaining section of hair and gather it into a high ponytail on the side or top of your head. Use a hair tie to secure it tightly and neatly.
  • Once done, release the first section of hair and create a second ponytail on the other end.
  • Take one ponytail and twist it tightly or braid it (this solely depends on your liking). Continue twisting or braiding until you reach the end of the hair and secure it with another hair tie.
  • Take the twisted or braided ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie, forming a bun. Secure it in place with bobby pins, making sure it feels secure and neat.
  • Repeat this step on the other end and create a matching bun.
  • Gently tug on the buns to make them fuller or messier, depending on the desired look. Use hairspray or styling products to tame any flyaways or add extra hold if needed.
  • You can decorate your space buns with accessories like ribbons, clips, or flowers to enhance the overall look. This step is entirely optional.
