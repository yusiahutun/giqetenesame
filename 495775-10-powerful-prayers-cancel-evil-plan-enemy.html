Catholic prayer to cancel the evil plan of the enemy

Catholic prayers are particular and take a precise tone. Catholics can pray from their heart or read prayers from novena books and other divine materials the church provides. One of the popular catholic prayers one can pray when they want to cancel their enemy's evil plan is the prayer to ST. Michael the Archangel.

Another prayer, which may not necessarily mention protection, but is essential for catholic worshipers, is the Act of Contrition. There are different versions of this prayer, which are key guides when praying for self and others.

Prayer to St. Michael the Archangel

St. Michael the Archangel, defend us in battle. Be our defence against the wickedness and snares of the devil. May God rebuke him, we humbly pray, and do thou, O Prince of the heavenly hosts, by the power of God, thrust into hell Satan and all the evil spirits, who prowl about the world seeking the ruin of souls. Amen.

Act of Contrition (traditional)
