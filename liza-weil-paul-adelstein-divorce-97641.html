Another Hollywood marriage bites the dust!

Gilmore Girls star Liza Weil, 38, and her actor husband, Paul Adelstein, 46, have filed for divorce after nine years of marriage, E! News confirmed on Monday, April 4.

The former couple are parents to a five-year-old daughter, Josephine, and hope to share custody of their little girl in the future.
