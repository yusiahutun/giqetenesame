1. Morgan Matthews

Who is the girl in the GMC Sierra commercial? Morgan Matthews is famous for her stellar role in the seasonal GMC commercial. The GMC Sierra commercial has been airing since 2018.

Morgan is from Nebraska, and she discovered her love for acting as a kid. She graduated from the University of Nebraska with a B.A. in Broadcast Journalism. She then studied acting with Margie Haber and Annie Grindlay's acting studio.

As an actress, she has appeared in Rizzoli & Isles, Super Pumped, Grey's Anatomy and several award-winning short films. Morgan was also an Improv troupe member for two years and hosted an exciting TV show, AXN Avenue, aired in Japan.

2. Will Arnett

Will Arnett is a famous American-Canadian voice actor, actor and comedian. He has been the voice of GMC since 1998 and was the voice-over actor in the GMC commercial.

3. Helena Mattsson

Helena Mattson, a Swedish-American actress, is one of the most established actresses in the GMC Sierra commercial. She has been featured in several GMC commercials but is famous for the puppy commercial.

Helena began acting at a young age, performing in concerts, musicals and plays. Immediately after graduating from Performing Arts School in Stockholm, Helena went to New York and London to continue studying acting.

Her quest paid off as she got recurring roles on TV series and shows such as The Rookie, CW's Nikita, Desperate Housewives, 666 Park Avenue and more. She's currently on the popular series Paper Empire as one of the star characters.

4. Rachelle Wood

Rachelle Wood was born in Glendale, California. Her first acting gig was a referral by a friend she had schooled with. In 1996, one of her close friends was on an NBC sitcom, Something So Right, and the producers asked her to find a friend who could feature in some episodes. That's how Rachelle got her first Hollywood taste.

She went on to feature in some minor films and also did modelling. Rachelle then moved to New York and studied acting at the Stella Adler Academy. It shaped her perfectly, and she came back ready for Hollywood.

Rachelle Wood has made several TV appearances, including commercials, films, and more. Some of the commercials she has appeared in include GMC, Reebok, Revlon, Nintendo etc. She has also had roles in movies and series such as Bad Blood, How I Met Your Mother, Rules of Engagement, Ghosts of Girlfriends Past etc.

5. Daniel Di Tomasso

Daniel Di Tomasso is the guy in the GMC truck commercial. He has been featured in GMC commercials such as Guy Thing and One for You, One for Me. Daniel Di Tomasso gifts Morgan Matthews a red GMC Sierra model in the GMC Sierra commercial.

The professional Canadian actor has starred in famous TV series and films, including Beauty and The Beast, Witches of the East End, and Good Girls Revolt.
