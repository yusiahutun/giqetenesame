Cheers to 15 Years at Taste of Little Italy

The Taste of Little Italy will return in its fifteenth year in Tradition Square in Port St. Lucie February 18-20. The cultural festival known for its inviting Italian ambience, national Italian-American entertainers, and authentic food will offer free admission in honor of its anniversary to thank the community for its continued support. Festival hours are 4-10 p.m. on Friday; 11 a.m. to 10 p.m. on Saturday; and 11 a.m. to 8 p.m. on Sunday. Parking is free.

The Taste of Little Italy, presented by Galbani Cheese, promises a weekend filled with entertainment, food, and fun, all served up Italian-style! Each year, the festival draws families from across the Treasure Coast with the promise creating memories that last a lifetime.

“As I reflected on the past 15 years, my heart is filled with gratitude for the way in which our community received The Taste of Little Italy,” said Jerry Somma, the festival’s founder and producer. “Knowing all the hardships that we all experienced through COVID, I felt it was important to show how thankful we are, that we are here for you, and we are one community together.”

Experience national and local chefs that come out to the Taste of Little Italy to demonstrate how to cook specialty Italian dishes. This year, the La Cucina Galbani Cooking stage will take the main spot, leading to lawn seating for everyone to enjoy. The Galbani “kitchen” will feature renowned chefs like Galbani’s own celebrity chef Marco Sciortino from Come Dine with Me, and local favorites Anthony Acampa and Pasquale Lamarra of Meeting Street Steak & Seafood and Pastaio. 

This year, guests can reserve party options with a Private VIP Villa located near the stage featuring a large table, ten seats, a complimentary bottle of wine, and a server. Alternatively, guests can enjoy reserved pod seating with four chairs, table, and a server.

Visitors will enjoy nonstop entertainment by DJ Anthony Mangini, Angelo Venuto, The Original Studio 54 Band, Austin Giorgio, The Mystics, and more.
