Inside DJ Khaleds Iced Out luxury watch collection: from rare Rolexes and Audemars Piguet R

He’s also an avid collector of luxury watches with an impressive and extensive collection that’s worth millions – and even possesses rare and custom timepieces by elite brands Rolex, Patek Philippe and Jacob & Co.

Here are some of the most valuable watches in DJ Khaled’s collection:

1. Jacob & Co. Billionaire III

In March, DJ Khaled posted an Instagram story with him wearing an attention-grabbing Jacob & Co. Billionaire watch. It’s already common knowledge that the music artist and producer owns another Billionaire III, adorned with 713 white diamonds and containing over 129 carats in total. It’s valued at a staggering US$3 million – his most expensive watch.

But this newest piece, which features a distinct gold band and setting, doesn’t appear to be available to the general public – meaning it’s quite literally priceless.

2. Jacob & Co. Astronomia Tourbillon Baguette

Khaled’s fondness for Jacob & Co. watches doesn’t end there. He’s frequently seen sporting various models from this innovative brand, whether in his music videos, at industry events, or while jet-setting around the world.

A particular highlight in Khaled’s collection is the Jacob & Co. Astronomia Tourbillon Baguette in White Gold. This space-themed watch created a buzz in the horology world upon its release. True to his luxurious taste, Khaled’s version of this watch is exceptionally opulent, set with 133 triangle-cut white diamonds, adding up to about 16.80 carats, and carrying a hefty price tag of US$1 million.

3. Patek Philippe Nautilus “The Chandelier”

This Miami music icon is also the owner of several Patek Philippes, specifically the Nautilus models. Among these is the Nautilus 5719/10G-010, a model he’s often seen wearing.

This particular watch, released in honour of the Nautilus’s 40th anniversary, is affectionately known as “The Chandelier” – and with good reason. It lives up to its name, featuring an extravagant setting of baguette diamonds across its dial, bezel and bracelet. It can fetch as much as US$800,000, though DJ Khaled’s Anniversary Edition, known for its unique “big face”, might even surpass the million-dollar mark, as suggested by the superstar himself to GQ.

4. Rolex Daytona “Eye of the Tiger”

At the 2023 Grammy Awards, Khaled turned heads with an exquisite Rolex Daytona “Eye of the Tiger” in yellow gold. This distinctive and rare watch is characterised by a tiger stripe pattern on the dial, with diamond hour markers and a bezel embellished with 36 trapeze-cut diamonds. The price tag for this exotic accessory? Around US$215,000.

5. Rolex Day-Date Platinum with Arabic Numerals and Calendar

The son of Palestinian immigrants and a devout Muslim, Khaled takes immense pride in his cultural roots. That pride is reflected in this highly unique platinum Rolex Day-Date, distinguished by its Arabic numbers and calendar dial.

The exquisite ice blue dial paired with Arabic numerals enhances the watch’s allure, making it a highly coveted and rare item on the grey market. The watch was specially selected and acquired for Khaled by his long-time jeweller, Pristine Jewellers, for around US$80,000, but is worth around US$150,000 today.

6. Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Frosted Gold

Khaled’s collection also includes several Royal Oaks by Audemars Piguet, and during his first visit to Paris in early 2023, he was seen wearing a stand-out piece: the Audemars Piguet Royal Oak “Rainbow” Frosted Gold Double Balance Wheel Openworked Ref. 15412BA.

This watch is celebrated for its exquisitely finished skeleton dial and a bezel set with rainbow gems. The unique frosted finish on the case and bracelet sets it apart from other timepieces, and justifies its estimated US$650,000 price tag on the secondary market.
