Dua Lipa's drastic new hair has fans questioning their own choices

After restarting her social media account, Dua Lipa is back with a bang, as she continues to show off her stunning new red hair.

The music star wiped her entire Instagram feed at the start of last month, returning a few weeks later with her new look. Fans suspected she was ready for a ‘new era’ with the release of her third album in the works. Although she’s had many eras, from the fan comments, it seems like her red-haired one is her best yet.

Dua Lipa stuns in red-haired selfie

Taking to her 88.6 million Instagram followers, the New Rules singer shared a carousel of images and videos to promote her surprise launch events across the globe.

In celebration of her new single, Houdini, the music star is putting on events in London, Los Angeles, and Tokyo.

Although fans were thrilled about the events, most of them just couldn’t get over a stunning new ‘natural’ selfie she shared, with her long cherry red locks stealing the show.

“She looks gorgeous in every hair color,” penned one.

Another wrote: “Most beautiful female artist of our generation.”

Upon seeing her stunning locks, many fans were also questioning their hair color choices:

“It’s that time of year where I argue with myself daily about dying my hair back red,” commented one.

“Me soon hopefully,” wrote another.

Another questioned: “Should I – no – but should I.”

The star has had a range of hair colors throughout the years

As fans say she ‘slays’ any color, we thought we’d take a look back at the 28-year-old’s hair transformations over the years.

When she first came onto the scene in 2017, she had her natural brown hair color, before cutting her long locks into a bob.

In 2019, she had a huge hair transformation when she unveiled a light blonde look.

She also went much darker, as she dyed her hair jet black, and we’ve even seen her blue as she starred as a mermaid in the Barbie movie.

Although she stuns in all variations, it looks like fans are rooting for the red to stay.
