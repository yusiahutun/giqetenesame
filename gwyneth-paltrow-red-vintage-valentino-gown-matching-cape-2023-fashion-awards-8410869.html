Gwyneth Paltrow Looked Festive in a Red Vintage Valentino Gown and Matching Sheer Cape

We sound like a broken record at this point, but red was the color of the fall, and it looks like the trend is sticking around for the winter months (or the holidays, at the very least). Case in point? Between the Academy Museum Gala and the 2023 Fashion Awards, the stars have been out and red was undoubtedly a recurring theme on the carpets (see: Kendall Jenner, Angela Bassett, Greta Lee, etc.)

Another star to commit to the shade was Gwyneth Paltrow, who looked both festive and fashion-forward during her own monochromatic red moment. Paltrow arrived on the carpet of the 2023 Fashion Awards in a vintage Valentino haute couture set that consisted of a strapless red gown with a keyhole cutout layered under a fuzzy, sheer, floor-skimming duster that was also adorned with beads.

Ornate diamond earrings, a red bedazzled braided cuff from Van Cleef & Arpels, and crimson pointed-toe pumps completed her outfit. She opted for a simple glam look that included straight hair that she tucked behind her ears, a dewy complexion, and a subtle mauve lip.

Paltrow gave her followers a closer look at the archival ensemble with a mirror selfie posted to her Instagram Story. Paltrow posed in front of a shelves while holding her glasses in one hand and her iPhone in the other, which she used to snap the photo. Her Story also featured other images from her trip to London, including a shot of her food at the famed restaurant Gymkhana and a shot of her husband, Brad Falchuk, from behind.
