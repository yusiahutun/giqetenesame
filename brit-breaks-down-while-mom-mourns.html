While her daughter Britney was being released from the hospital, Lynne Spears was 3,000 miles away at a cemetery in Kentwood, La. today, laying flowers at the grave of her sister.

Britney's aunt, Sandra Bridges Covington, died last January of ovarian cancer. Britney attended the funeral.
