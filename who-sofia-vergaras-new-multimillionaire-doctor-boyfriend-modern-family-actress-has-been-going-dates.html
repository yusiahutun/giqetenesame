He’s an entrepreneur

Saliman’s talents extend beyond the operating room.

Per his profile, he founded Ceterix Orthopaedics and is the inventor of the NovoStitch Pro Meniscal Repair System. The latter was acquired by medical device company Smith & Nephew in 2019 for a whopping US$105 million, according to Forbes.

He has a strong vision for the medical industry. “I can have more of an impact on humanity by solving a need in the medtech space than I ever could treating individual patients.
