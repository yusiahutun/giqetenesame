High top dreads styles ideas for men

When it comes to appearance, the bottom line is that you will look as good as you try. And as you know, grooming is essential to a man's outward appearance. If you fancy having long hair, you must style it and try giving it that unique touch.

Dreadlocks are common with men, more so high top dreads. However, you do not want to look like you have a generic style. 1. Long dreadlocks with undercut

Looking unique is all about trying something different. And if you have dreads, you can style them however you desire. The long dreads with an undercut is a good idea because it doesn't require much effort. This style is for men with significantly longer and thicker high top dreads.

2. Short dreadlocks with bald fade

The short dreads with bald fade is one of the most common high top dreads styles for men. Many men like short dreadlocks because of the ease of styling and less stress on your head. As you know, dreads can become voluminous if left to grow and become a burden to your neck because of the weight.

4. High top ponytail with undercut

A high top ponytail is quite common with dreadlocks. You can easily create this look by gathering your long dread concentrated on the top of your head into a high ponytail. And that is it. Ascertain that the ponytail is well-secured, probably with a band. Of course, the hair must be short on the sides and tapered to undercut.
