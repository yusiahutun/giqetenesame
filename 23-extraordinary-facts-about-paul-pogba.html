Paul Pogba was born on March 15, 1993.

Paul Pogba, the renowned French footballer, was born on March 15, 1993, in Lagny-sur-Marne, a commune in the eastern suburbs of Paris. His birth sign is Pisces.

Pogba comes from a family of footballers.
