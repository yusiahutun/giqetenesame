A Dimensional Visitor

Kartana is an Ultra Beast that originates from a different dimension known as the Ultra Space. Its arrival in the Pokémon world brings both mystery and excitement.

The Paper-Thin Pokémon

Kartana’s body is extremely thin and lightweight, resembling a delicate sheet of paper. However, its strength and resilience are far from fragile, making it a formidable opponent in battles.

The Grass/Steel Type Combination

Kartana possesses a dual Grass/Steel typing, making it resistant to several types of attacks. This unique combination enhances its defense and enables it to stand up against various opponents.

Legendary Origins

Kartana is one of the legendary Pokémon belonging to the Ultra Beasts, a group of powerful and mysterious creatures that hail from the Ultra Space.

Ultra Beast ID Number

In the Pokédex, Kartana is identified as Ultra Beast number 798, emphasizing its significance and rarity.

Blade-Like Appendages

One of Kartana’s most striking features is its sharp, blade-like arms. These appendages are incredibly sharp and can effortlessly slice through even the toughest materials.

Stealthy Speed

Despite its slender frame, Kartana is exceptionally fast and agile. It can swiftly maneuver through battles, often catching opponents off guard with its quick movements.

The Beast Boost Ability

Kartana possesses the special ability called Beast Boost. This ability allows Kartana’s highest stat to increase after successfully defeating an opponent, further enhancing its power as the battle progresses.

Tactical Precision

Kartana’s precision in battle is unparalleled. It calculates every move with meticulous accuracy, ensuring each strike lands perfectly to maximize its chances of victory.

Guardians of the Alola Region

Kartana, along with the other Ultra Beasts, plays a significant role in protecting the Alola region from impending threats. Its presence adds an extra layer of intrigue to the Pokémon lore.

The Cut Ability

With its sharp blades, Kartana can effortlessly cut through objects and obstacles. This ability comes in handy during adventures and can be utilized to access new areas.

The Legendary Tapu’s Adversary

Kartana’s appearance and incredible strength make it a formidable adversary for the legendary Guardian Pokémon known as Tapu Bulu. The clash between these legendary creatures is an epic battle to behold.

Kartana’s Great Defense

Despite its offensive prowess, Kartana also possesses impressive defensive capabilities. Its steel typing grants it resistance against several damaging moves, enabling it to withstand powerful attacks.

Oblivious to Distraction

Kartana remains focused and unperturbed by any distractions on the battlefield. Its unwavering concentration allows it to maintain peak performance and execute devastating moves.

Mythical Status

Kartana’s status as a legendary Pokémon places it in the mythical realm of Pokémon lore. Trainers around the world seek to capture and harness its immense power.

Ultra Warp Ride Encounter

In Pokémon Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon, trainers can encounter Kartana by riding an Ultra Beast through the Ultra Wormholes. This thrilling experience adds an extra layer of excitement to the game.

The Master of Swords Dance

Kartana is a master of the Swords Dance move, which significantly increases its attack power. When combined with its already formidable offense, this move can swiftly dismantle opponents in battle.

Pokedex Entry

The Pokédex entry for Kartana describes it as a “sword with a soul” and emphasizes its swift, precise movements that can slice through anything in its path.

Legendary Statue in Ultra Megalopolis

In Pokémon Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon, trainers can find a statue honoring Kartana in Ultra Megalopolis. This statue serves as a reminder of its legendary status and power.

A Symbol of Balance

Kartana’s presence in the Pokémon world represents a delicate balance between strength and fragility. Its paper-thin body houses immense power, symbolizing that appearances can be deceiving.
