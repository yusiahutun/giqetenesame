Suri Cruise enjoys a day out at the beach with mum Katie Holmes

    Suri Cruise, the fashionable daughter of Katie Holmes and Tom Cruise, was spotted having a whale of a time at Westhampton Beach, Long Island where she spent the week with her family.The seven-year-old had a huge smile on her face all day as she played in the sand and splashed around in the waves.Suri was learning to surf on a boogie board, and even though the pint-sized fashionista fell off a few times, she couldn't stop laughing and appeared to be having a lot of fun in the sun.

    The little girl lived up to her fashionable reputation, and looked pretty in pink wearing a rose-printed bathing suit ensemble with pink bows.

    Her mum Katie, 34, looked just as ready for the sunshine and matched her daughter in a bright yellow beach dress with crochet flowers.

    The family were out for the day and joined by other friends and relatives who took some relief from the sun under big, blue and yellow umbrellas.

    Katie, who has been busy filming romantic comedy , will have welcomed her beach holiday and quality time with her daughter.The pair were spotted enjoying a dose of rest and relaxation in the sunny shores of Florida in May when they celebrated Memorial Day weekend.The family once again headed for the beach and enjoyed a low-key couple of days, eating at local eateries and browsing the small shops.
