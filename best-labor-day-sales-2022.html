44 Best Labor Day Sales 2022

As the last long stretch of summer looms on the horizon, a slew of Labor Day fashion sales follows in its wake. It's the best kind of send-off summer a discount shopper could ever wish for.

Before you bring out your favorite sweaters, blazers, and other long-missed layers, spend this holiday weekend adding those final must-have fall items to your wardrobe thanks to our curated list of the 44 top Labor Day fashion deals. Be it a pair of over-the-knee boots your wardrobe can't do without or your next best pair of vintage-inspired denim from the minds over at Everlane, it's time for some autumn dressing.

Take advantage of being out of office and check out the best Labor Day deals available below. But don't scroll ahead too quickly, for you might miss some last-minute summer finds from department store favorites such as Nordstrom and SSENSE, with discounts up to 80 percent off. Price cuts this special will be gone before you know it, so don't miss out.
