The 10 Best Cherry Perfumes to Shop in 2024

Here's an eau de parfum that's inspired by a Vivienne Westwood store in London called Sex, Cherry Punk. The fragrance encapsulates the best of the underground punk scene, nostalgia of vinyl record shops, and innocence of adolescence. It starts off with cherry and Sichuan pepper top notes, followed by a floral heart of violet, jasmine absolute, and mimosa. And later, it dries down with notes of leather and tonka bean.

One reviewer writes: "Run don’t walk to get this fragrance. I don’t usually enjoy cherry notes, but the combination of this with leather, patchouli, and Tonka just makes it so deep and intense. I can’t stop smelling it." —Divine Mrs M.
