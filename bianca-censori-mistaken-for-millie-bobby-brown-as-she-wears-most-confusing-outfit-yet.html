Bianca Censori 'mistaken for Millie Bobby Brown' as she wears 'most confusing outfit yet'

Just days after switching it up and covering herself in a big fur coat whilst out with Kanye and North West, Bianca Censori is back at it with obscure fashion choices, and her newest blue bodysuit may be the most confusing yet. So confusing, some were mistaking her for someone else.

Ever since Kanye West and Bianca Censori got together, his wife has been making headlines for her fashion choices, which many believe are strictly down to Ye. His Instagram has gained tons of attention in that time, as he regularly posts NSFW pics of his wife. Now, the paps do that job for him as lately, it seems like we can’t go one day without seeing a bit too much of Bianca…

Bianca Censori steps out in ‘confusing’ blue bodysuit and white tights

The celebrity couple were having a normal date night, stepping out to the theatre to watch Dune 2, featuring Zendaya and Timothee Chalamet.

Although the music star was appropriately dressed for the theatre, with his usual black hoodie and black pants, his wife was not.

In one of her most ‘mismatched’ outfits yet, the former Yeezy employee stepped out in a low-cut blue body suit which was cut out at the back.

Instead of wearing a pair of pants or jeans over the bodysuit, Bianca opted for a pair of white tights to wear underneath, rounding the look off with a pair of white heels.

Her hair was scraped back into a bun, so her top was on full display. The architect looked in good spirits as she could be seen cracking some smiles and laughs as she held her husband’s hand.

“For a minute I thought that was Millie Brown,” wrote one fan on X, formerly known as Twitter, in reference to her Stranger Things character, Eleven.

“Interesting outfit choice for the movies,” penned another.

The star ‘covered up’ while out with stepdaughter North West

Despite being known for her ‘revealing’ looks, the 29-year-old stepped out covered head to toe with a fur jacket and black leggings while out to dinner with Ye and his eldest daughter, North West.

Once again, the rapper was dressed in his black hoodie and pants, while North sported a black Raiders jersey.

It comes after reports suggested Kim Kardashian didn’t want Censori to dress how she normally does, around the kids.

“Kim instructed Kanye to never let Bianca dress like that around their kids. She is truly surprised that Kanye would let his wife leave the house like that,” a source told the Daily Mail.

However, the two looked on good terms as they ran into each other during Ye’s listening party, and it looks like Bianca even took some fashion advice from Kim as she was spotted wearing a ‘SKIMS dupe’ just last week.

Although some may think Ye’s wife looks ‘unrecognizable’ covered up, it’s not the first time. At the start of the year, she was seen in a huge faux fur coat, covering much more than needed, so much so, you could barely see her eyes.

One extreme to another!
