The 16 Best Wellness Brands to Shop in 2024

Approved by celebrities, influencers, and Bazaar editors alike, meal-plan brand Sakara’s healthy offerings include organic, ready-to-eat entrées, snacks, and more that are anything but boring. The flexible and prescriptive plan menus change weekly, and include a range of mouthwatering plant-based dishes that are rich in nutrients—and frequently graced with an abundance of crisp, leafy greens. Choose up to three meal selections per day (breakfast, lunch, and dinner) and up to five full days of deliveries.

In addition to the meal service, the brand also features add-ons like vitamins and powders to help boost the impact of the nutrient-dense plates. Depending on how many meals and delivery days you add to your plan, the program can cost anywhere from $56 to $420 per week.

Price range: $16 to $420 per item/meal plan
