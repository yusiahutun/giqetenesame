Carl Thompson Jr. | Obituaries

Carl Thompson Jr. of Colorado Springs, Colo., and formerly of Laurel, died in Colorado Springs. Funeral services will be Saturday March 7, at 10 a.m. at New Testament Church in the Currie Community with burial in Oak Hill No. 2 Cemetery. Visitation will be from 2-5 p.m. Friday in the chapel. Brown Mortuary Inc. is in charge of the arrangements.
