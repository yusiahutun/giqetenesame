Pastor Rose Shaboka Slams Stingy Wives Who Don't Buy Food for Family, Clothes for Hubbies: &quot;Mungu Aw

  • Rose is not only the wife of Pastor Nick Shaboka of New Day Church, she is also a co-pastor and founder of Winning Women International Ministry
  • The beautiful Tanzanian pastor is known to teach on relationship and family matters both online and offline
  • She is now calling upon women to stop being selfish and help solve the problems in the family

Tanzanian preacher Rose Shaboka has called out selfish women who don't contribute anything to the family kitty.

Shaboka said wives don't buy food

Speaking in an Instagram video, Shaboka said some women are very selfish as whenever the kids ask for food, they don't give but will never hesitate to buy clothes for different occasions.

"You say your father has not left any money for food while you have hidden some money somewhere," she chastised the women.
