'Happy Days' Actor Henry Winkler's Longtime and Loyal Married Life with Wife Stacey Weitzman

If you take care of things, it lasts. It is true not only for an object but also for a relationship. The relationship between Henry Winkler, Happy Days actor, and Stacey Weitzman is a living example of it.

Even though their first meeting looked coincidental, the couple went to have a successful relationship later on. To know about their first encounter, marriage, and children; read further below:

Henry Winkler's Longtime and Loyal Married Life with Stacey Weitzman

The loyal man Henry Winkler is successful in keeping his married life together for 40 years and seems likely until the end. The couple got married on May 5, 1978, and since then, they have stayed together.

Henry Winkler Married his wife Stacey Weitzman.

The couple still loves each other as if they are in the '70s. The story of them dating back in the '70s is so intriguing. Once Fonzie, Henry Winkler, walked into a clothing store in L.A to buy a sports coat. He asked for help and Stacey Weitzman appeared by. And that's how they met for the first time.

Henry Winkler's Children with Stacey Weitzman

Henry has two children with Stacey Weitzman, Zoe Emily (born 1980) and Max Daniel (born 1983). Winkler also has a stepson, Jed Weitzman from Stacey Weitzman's previous marriage.

Henry with Zoe Emily and Max Daniel. Source: Oprah

The relationship between his son and daughter has always been good with Winkler. Zoe once said that, until she was 16 and got her driving license, Winkler always drove her to school.
