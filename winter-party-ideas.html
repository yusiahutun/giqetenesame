Winter Party Ideas - Recipes and Ideas for a Snow Day Party

Kate Martin, an event planner, views even the most laid-back gatherings as a chance to experiment. In the case of this sledding party with her family and friends, that means mitten-shaped invites, a tablecloth transformed by snowflake stencils, a hot-chocolate bar, and a snack station groaning with treats: cookies, s'mores, popcorn, and more. At every turn, thoughtful details show care without trying to overimpress. Flip through the following slides to throw your own snow-day sledding party.
