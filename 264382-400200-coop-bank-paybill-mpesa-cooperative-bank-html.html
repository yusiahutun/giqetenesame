Co-op Bank Paybill: MPESA to Cooperative Bank 400200 and 400222

The banking industry has changed drastically in the last few decades. In the past, people had to visit bank branches to initiate and complete transactions. Today, you can send money instantly using the Co-op Bank paybill number. The use of mobile money has made life a lot easier.

Did you know you can use the Co-op Bank paybill number to send money instantly? With this technology, you can withdraw, send, or deposit money from one bank account to another in the comfort of your living room or office.

All about the Co-op Bank Paybill number

The Co-operative Bank of Kenya Limited, commonly known as Co-op Bank, is a leading financial institution in Kenya. Initially, it was registered under the Co-operative Societies Act.

In 2008, it was incorporated under the Companies Act. The institution then went public and was listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE). The financial institution has kept abreast with technological changes, including mobile banking.

Co-operative Bank paybill number

Co-op Bank has two official paybill numbers, as listed below.

  • Co-operative Bank bank paybill 1: 400200 (money transfer)
  • Co-op paybill 2: 400222 (MCollection solution)

How to send money from M-Pesa to Co-operative Bank

If you wish to send cash into any Co-op Bank account via M-Pesa, you should use the Co-op paybill number 400200. Follow the steps below to learn how to do this.

  • Open your M-Pesa app, MySafaricom app, or your SIM tool kit.
  • Go to Lipa Na M-pesa, then select the Paybill option.
  • Enter M-Pesa to Co-op Bank business number, i.e., 400200.
  • Enter the Co-op account number you wish to deposit the money.
  • Enter the amount you wish to deposit.
  • Enter your M-Pesa PIN and confirm the transaction.
  • Expect a confirmation SMS from M-Pesa immediately.
  • Await a second confirmation SMS from Co-operative Bank containing the details of the deposit.

How to deposit money from M-Pesa to Co-operative Bank: MCollection solution

The MCollection solution is ideal for businesses or institutions that receive bulk payments from customers via M-Pesa, e.g., school fees payments, rent from tenants, e.t.c. This option allows businesses to track their finances better.

The MCollection solution paybill number is 400222. Customers should follow the steps below to deposit money from M-Pesa to your account.

  • Open your M-Pesa app, MySafaricom app, or your SIM tool kit.
  • Go to Lipa Na M-pesa, then select the Paybill option.
  • Enter the paybill number 400222.
  • Enter the account number, i.e., your MCollection Business Code followed by ‘#’. An example is 123456#. Customers may also include their names or customer number, e.g., 123456#Hse24 or 123456#Mary.
  • Enter the amount.
  • Enter M-Pesa PIN and confirm the transaction.
  • Both the customer and business owner will receive a confirmation SMS.

Features and benefits of MCollection solution

As mentioned earlier, this option is suitable for accounts that receive payments from multiple clients. It allows for better management of funds. Below are the features and benefits of MCollection solution.

  • There is no registration fee for the service.
  • No commission is charged when you receive money.
  • It is easier to reconcile accounts because customers can include their name or customer number.
  • Clients and customers receive email/ SMS notifications for each payment.
  • Payments reflect in your account instantly.

How to apply for MCollection solution

Businesses or accounts that receive bulk payments from multiple clients can only use this service with registration. Follow the steps below to register for this service.

  • Visit your nearest Co-op branch and fill in an MCollection registration form.
  • The relevant officials will process your request, and you will be given your business code on the same day.
  • Once you are issued the code, you can start receiving M-Pesa payments immediately.

Paybill charges

The paybill numbers 400200 and 40222 do not attract any charges. This means that customers can transfer money from M-Pesa to any Co-op account free of charge.

Is Co-op paybill 400200 or 400222?

Both numbers are correct. 400200 is used to send cash into any Co-op Bank account via M-Pesa, while 400222 is specifically for accounts that receive payments from multiple clients.

What is the Co-operative Bank paybill number?

The Lipa Na M-Pesa paybill number is 400200 for people who wish to send cash into any Co-op Bank account via M-Pesa, or 400222 for accounts that receive payments from multiple clients, e.g., businesses.

How do I deposit money from M-Pesa to Co-op Bank?

You can deposit using the Lipa Na M-Pesa option on your M-Pesa app, MySafaricom app, or your SIM tool kit. The paybill number to use can either be 400200 or 400222.
