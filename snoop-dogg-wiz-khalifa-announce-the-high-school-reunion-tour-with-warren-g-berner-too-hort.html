Snoop Dogg &amp; Wiz Khalifa Announce The High School Reunion Tour With Warren G, Berner, &amp; Too $h

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – The original D-O-Double G, Snoop Dogg has announced the High School Reunion Tour with Baltimore’s son, Wiz Khalifa, Too $hort, the “Regulator” Warren G, and Berner – with special guest DJ Drama.

The Live Nation (LN) produced 33-city tour kicks off Friday (July 7) at Vancouver’s Rogers Arena and hits Brooklyn, Atlanta, Houston, Bristow, and more before wrapping up at Irvine’s FivePoint Amphitheatre on Saturday (August 27).

The Citi presale began today (March 7) with the general on-sale beginning Friday (March 10) via ticketmaster.com. Fans can also purchase VIP packages, which may include premium tickets, access to the VIP lounge, limited edition numbered poster, specially designed VIP gift items, and more via VIPnation.com.
