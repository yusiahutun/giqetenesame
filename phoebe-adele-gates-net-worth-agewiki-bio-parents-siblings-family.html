Family, Siblings

Melinda Gates who is popular as Bill Gates is an American businesswoman and a famous philanthropist and especially Bill Gates’ wife. Although better known as Bill Gates’ wife she established her own name by being actively involved in social causes.

Melinda met Bill Gates as an employee at Microsoft she stopped working for Microsoft after the birth of her first child to take care of her family.
