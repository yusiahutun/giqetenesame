Nicah the Queen Reignites Feud with Amber Ray Online, Labels Her Husband Snatcher

  • Amber Ray and Nicah the Queen threw shades at each other on Instagram, and it is not clear why they are beefing
  • It all started with comedian Dr Ofweneke's ex-wife branding Amber a husband snatcher, urging women to always pray for their husbands
  • The gospel singer told women by praying their husbands would not fall into temptation to have an affair with Amber
  • The controversial socialite savagely responded to Nicah, telling her to stop judging her, and even referred to Christian teachings about judging others

There seems to be beef brewing between gospel musician Nicah the Queen and socialite Amber Ray.

Nicah vs Amber

It all started with Nicah, comedian Dr Ofweneke's ex-wife branding Amber a husband snatcher, urging women to always pray for their husbands so that they would not fall into temptation with the socialite.

"This is a notice from Nicah to my fellow women/wives! Kindly let's put our husbands in prayers like never before. Sasa imagine ukose kumuombea atoke hapa nje apatane na Amber Ray akuabishe in public ata ukimsamehe kila mtu anajua. So let's pray for our husbands," she wrote.

The mother of two further shared a photo of Amber allegedly at a witch doctor's shack, warning women to be careful, insisting they pray harder.

Nicah's sentiments didn't sit well with the controversial socialite and she fired back.

"Even when laziness is hidden behind prayers, it still shows when your husband leaves you for a hard worker and end up starting amazing shows like Dr. Ofweneke live," she said.

The gospel singer retaliated and she said:

"Hebu mnisaidie kudefine (help me define) hardwork. What are some of the jobs people do to pay bills? Pray for your husbands kuna hardwork inapangwa mahali."

"Our desperate search for godliness has caused narcissism to embody even the highest attainment of Christian leadership.
