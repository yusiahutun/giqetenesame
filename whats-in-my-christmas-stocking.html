Whats in my Christmas stocking?

<p>So I have seen all my children’s plays - and they were all great, the little angels! They love being on stage and are natural to the core. I have finished my Christmas shopping, and I made sure I enjoyed it this year, not making it a rush or a chore. I have wrapped all the presents and they are all ready to be opened and are sitting patiently under my tree. <p>I cooked my first Christmas dinner already (veggie of course) it’s a tradition that I have a dinner with Mairead Nash and Nick Grimshaw, we three, do it every year! We had it early because Mairead is going away for what seems like forever! She manages the band <a href="https://www.harpersbazaar.com/uk/fashion/florence-welch-at-chanel-ss-12-001">Florence and the Machine</a>, who are on a world tour, but the big bonus is that I get to go to Flo’s gigs and stand at the side of the stage and cheer! <p>So with all the plays, Iris’ choir performances and a visit to the theatre to see my dear friend Frances Ruffell who was starring in the play <em>Pippin</em> at The Chocolate Factory in London Bridge. Frances shone as usual. We met when we were 11 and grew up together and seeing her up there on the stage doing what she does best - singing, dancing and shining like the biggest star - I am very proud to be her friend. <p>I sat and wrote the poem below to sum up how I feel about Christmas and my loved ones. I hope you like it. Merry Christmas everybody! <p>This year,<br /> I don’t need anything<br /> in my Christmas stocking...</p> <p>Maybe just some air,<br /> maybe <br /> Just some extra breath <br /> exhaled by you.</p> <p>No toys, <br /> or sweets or gadgets<br /> need be in there,<br /> above the musty nuts and<br /> squashed satsumas,<br /> no baubles or chocolates<br /> or perfume.</p> <p>Just your love,<br /> wrapped up in an<br /> invisible parcel,<br /> just the breath<br /> that reminds me of you.</p> <p>The air that means more<br /> than anything else,<br /> no sweet fruits warm my heart<br /> like you do.</p> <p>The only other thing I ask,<br /> is that my stocking<br /> gives peace<br /> to those who need it;<br /> smiles for those that are sad<br /> and thawing for those that are bad.</p> <p>So, when I hang my sock<br /> above the hearth<br /> and wait for Santa to fall,<br /> I 'll ask for one last wish<br /> that is;<br /> happy Christmas to you all!</p>
